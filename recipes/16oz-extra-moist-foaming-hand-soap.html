16oz Extra Moist Foaming Hand Soap

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp liquid castile soap (unscented or gentle)
  • 1 1/2 cups (12 oz) distilled or cooled boiled water
  • 2 tsp sweet almond oil or jojoba oil
  • 1 tsp vegetable glycerin (optional, for hydration)
  • 1–2 tbsp aloe vera gel (optional, soothing)
  • 8–12 drops lavender or chamomile essential oil (gentle fragrance)

Directions

  1. 1. Add the liquid castile soap to a clean 16 oz foaming soap dispenser.
  2. Add the almond/jojoba oil, glycerin, and aloe vera gel if using.
  3. Add the essential oil drops.
  4. Slowly fill with distilled water, leaving 1–2 inches at the top.
  5. Screw on the pump and swirl gently to mix.
  6. Use as regular foaming hand soap.
