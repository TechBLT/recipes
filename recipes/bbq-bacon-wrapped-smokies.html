BBQ Bacon Wrapped Smokies

Source: https://cdn.ruled.me/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/bbqbaconwrappedsmokies.jpg

Ingredients

  • 24
  • Lit'l Smokies
  • 6
  • slices bacon
  • 3
  • tablespoons Ruled.me BBQ Sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. The Execution
  2. Preheat oven to 375F. Start by slices of bacon into quarter-pieces, so you have 24 quarter-slices of bacon. Place a little smokie on top of the slice of bacon.
  3. Roll the little smokies up in the bacon so that there is a small amount of overlap and stick a toothpick into them.
  4. Set on a cookie sheet covered with foil and bake for 25 minutes. After 25 minutes, baste little smokies with BBQ sauce and bake for another 10-12 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly, then serve.
