Asian Chicken for Pressure Canning

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/1ACCDC1A-40E0-4355-978F-5B7CECB8FFDE.jpg?resize=600%2C600&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 9
  • lbs Chicken, diced into 1 inch pieces
  • 5
  • Medium carrots, diced
  • 2
  • Large onions, chopped
  • 7
  • Cloves garlic, minced
  • 12
  • cups Chicken broth
  • 3/4
  • cup Soy sauce
  • 1
  • tbsp Onion powder
  • 1
  • tbsp Ground or fresh ginger
  • 1 1/2
  • tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/4 – 1/2
  • tsp Cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Wash jars, lids, rings in hot, soapy water. Prepare pressure canner per canner instructions, filling with water. Bring canner to a low simmer.
  2. Add chicken broth to pot with soy sauce, onion powder, garlic powder and ginger. Bring to a boil, cover, turn down heat and simmer 10 minutes or until ready to use.
  3. Divide chicken, onions and carrots among jars evenly. Add 1 minced clove of garlic to each jar and fill with broth leaving 1 inch headspace. De-bubble, wipe rimes, place lids and rings fingertip tight. Add jars to simmering pressure canner.
  4. Process quarts 90 minutes and pints for 75 minutes.
