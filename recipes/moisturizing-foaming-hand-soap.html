Moisturizing Foaming Hand Soap

Ingredients

  • 2
  • tbsp liquid castile soap (unscented or gentle)
  • 1–1.5
  • cups distilled or boiled & cooled water
  • 1
  • tsp sweet almond oil or jojoba oil (for moisture)
  • 5–10
  • drops lavender or tea tree essential oil (optional)

Directions

  1. 1. Add the liquid castile soap to a clean foaming soap dispenser.
  2. 2. Add the sweet almond or jojoba oil.
  3. 3. If desired, add 5–10 drops of essential oil for fragrance.
  4. 4. Slowly fill the rest of the dispenser with distilled water, leaving a little room at the top.
  5. 5. Screw on the pump and gently shake to combine.
  6. 6. Use as you would regular foaming soap.
