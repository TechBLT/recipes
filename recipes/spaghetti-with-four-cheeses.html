Spaghetti with Four Cheeses

Source: https://www.tasteofhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Spaghetti-with-Four-Cheeses_EXPS_FT19_39663_F_0919_1.jpg

Ingredients

  • 8
  • ounces uncooked spaghetti
  • 1/4
  • cup butter, cubed
  • 1
  • tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon pepper
  • 1-1/2
  • cups half-and-half cream
  • 1
  • cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 4
  • ounces fontina cheese, shredded
  • 1/2
  • cup shredded provolone cheese
  • 1/4
  • cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2
  • tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, melt butter. Stir in the flour, salt and pepper until smooth. Gradually stir in cream. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from the heat; stir in cheeses until melted.
  2. Drain spaghetti; toss with cheese sauce and parsley.
