Copycat Keto Chick-Fil-A Sauce

Source: https://ketoinpearls.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/IMG_0390-copy-100x100.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons yellow mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke
  • 2 teaspoons sugar free bbq sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon powdered monkfruit

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients in bowl or food processor. Whisk or pulse until combined. Store in airtight container in the refrigerator up to two weeks.
