Blackstone Greek Chicken and Potatoes

Source: https://ifyougiveagirlagrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/IMG_5147.jpg

Ingredients

  • cooking oil of choice (I used olive oil)
  • 1
  • pound petite redskin or gold potatoes diced, about inch size pieces
  • kosher salt, pepper, dried oregano
  • 1/2
  • cup water
  • 1 1/2
  • pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs (or breasts) cut into bite size pieces
  • 1
  • red onion diced
  • 1
  • red bell pepper sliced
  • 7 to 8
  • cloves garlic minced
  • 8
  • ounces fresh spinach roughly chopped
  • 1/4
  • cup lemon juice
  • 2
  • 5.3 ounce containers plain Greek yogurt
  • 6
  • ounces crumbled feta cheese
  • Optional: basil for garnish

Directions

  1. Prep all the ingredients and put them on a tray to carry out to the griddle.
  2. Preheat the griddle to medium heat for several minutes. Add some cooking oil of choice and the potatoes, season them with kosher salt, pepper, and oregano.
  3. Cook the potatoes on the griddle for 6 to 7 minutes, cover them with a melting dome as they cook, add some water under the dome to steam them. Flip them a few times with hibachi spatulas and add more water a few times.
  4. Note: If you don't have a melting dome, a foil pan works. Just be careful touching it as it will be hot.
  5. Remove the melting dome and add more cooking oil, the chicken, diced onions, sliced peppers, and season with more kosher salt, pepper, and oregano. Cook together with the potatoes for 6 to 7 more minutes, flipping and mixing a few times.
  6. Add the garlic and spinach. Cook 2 to 3 more minutes, flipping and mixing together on the griddle a few times.
  7. Turn the griddle off and add the lemon, yogurt, and half of the feta. Mix together for a minute and taste for seasoning, add more kosher salt, pepper, and/or oregano if needed. Garnish with the remaining feta and basil if desired.
