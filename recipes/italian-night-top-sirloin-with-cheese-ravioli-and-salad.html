Italian Night – Top Sirloin with Cheese Ravioli and Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 top sirloin steaks (about 8 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 package cheese ravioli
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Italian dressing

Directions

  1. 1. Pat steaks dry, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Cook steaks 3–4 min per side for medium rare. Add garlic and butter at the end, spoon over steaks. Rest 5 minutes.
  3. Cook ravioli according to package. Warm marinara in saucepan. Toss together and top with Parmesan.
  4. Toss salad greens, cherry tomatoes, and onion with Italian dressing.
  5. Serve steaks with ravioli and salad.
