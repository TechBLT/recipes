Best-Ever Instant Pot Mac & Cheese (BBQ Style)

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb elbow macaroni or cavatappi
  • 4
  • cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2
  • Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2
  • tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2
  • tsp black pepper
  • 1/2
  • tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2
  • tsp smoked paprika
  • 1
  • tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1
  • cup heavy cream (or 3/4 cup evaporated milk)
  • 2
  • cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 1/2
  • cups Gruyère cheese, shredded
  • 1
  • cup Monterey Jack or Fontina cheese, shredded
  • 1/2
  • cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Optional topping: 1 cup crushed Ritz crackers or panko + 2 Tbsp melted butter + pinch smoked paprika

Directions

  1. 1. Add pasta, broth, butter, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika to Instant Pot. Lock lid and set to Manual / High Pressure for 4 minutes. Quick release immediately when finished.
  2. 2. Stir in Dijon mustard and heavy cream. Pasta should look slightly loose with liquid.
  3. 3. Add cheeses one handful at a time, stirring constantly until fully melted and silky.
  4. 4. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve creamy as-is, or transfer to an oven-safe dish.
  5. 5. Optional: Top with Ritz/panko mixture and broil 2–3 minutes until golden and crunchy.
