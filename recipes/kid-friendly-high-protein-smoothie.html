Kid-Friendly High Protein Smoothie

Ingredients

  • ¾
  • cup unsweetened almond milk (or regular milk)
  • ½
  • frozen banana
  • ½
  • cup frozen strawberries (or blueberries/mixed berries)
  • 1
  • scoop vanilla whey or plant protein (low sugar)
  • 1–2
  • tsp natural peanut butter or almond butter (optional)
  • ½
  • tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 2–3
  • ice cubes
  • Optional: ½ tsp honey or maple syrup if more sweetness is needed

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to the single-serve container of your Shark blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed. Serve immediately and enjoy!
