Instant Pot Short Ribs

Source: https://www.pressurecookrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/instant-pot-short-ribs-recipe.jpg

Ingredients

  • 6 - 10
  • pieces (1116g) bone-in short ribs (1.5 - 2 inches thick)
  • 6
  • cloves garlic , crushed and chopped
  • 2
  • (300g) small onions , sliced
  • 2
  • (170g) medium carrots , chopped
  • 2
  • ribs (90g) celery , chopped
  • ½
  • cup (125ml) red wine (Cabernet Sauvignon)
  • ½
  • cup (125ml) unsalted chicken stock or good quality beef broth
  • 2
  • tablespoons (30ml) balsamic vinegar
  • 1 - 1.5
  • tablespoon (14-21g) brown sugar
  • 1
  • tablespoon (15ml) light or regular soy sauce (not low sodium soy sauce)
  • 1
  • tablespoon (15ml) fish sauce
  • 1
  • tablespoon (15ml) olive oil
  • 2
  • bay leaves
  • A pinch dried thyme
  • Kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Thickener:
  • 2.5
  • tablespoons (22.5g) cornstarch
  • 3
  • tablespoons (45ml) cold water
  • Mashed Potatoes (Optional):
  • 3 - 4
  • russet potatoes , halved
  • 100
  • ml whole milk
  • 1 - 2
  • tablespoons (14g - 28g) unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Brown Short Ribs in Instant Pot: Heat up Instant Pot using Sauté More. Wait until it says HOT (prevents short ribs from sticking to the pot). Add 1 tbsp (15ml) olive oil in Instant Pot. Season one side generously with salt and black pepper. Add in 5 pieces of short ribs, then brown each side for 4 minutes. While one side is browning, season the other side with more salt and black pepper.
  2. *Pro Tip: Avoid overcrowding by browning short ribs in batches.
  3. Saute Onion & Garlic: Set aside the browned short ribs. Add in sliced onions, and saute until soften (~ 3 minutes). Add in chopped garlic, and saute until fragrant (~30 seconds).
  4. Saute Veggies & Spices: Add in chopped carrots, celery, a pinch of dried thyme, and 2 bay leaves, then saute for 2 minutes.
  5. Deglaze Instant Pot: Pour ½ cup (125ml) red wine in Instant Pot and deglaze by scrubbing the flavorful brown bits off the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon. Give it a quick mix. Let the wine simmer for a minute to allow some of the alcohol to evaporate.
  6. Pressure Cook Short Ribs: Add 1 tbsp (15ml) light soy sauce, 1 tbsp (15ml) fish sauce, 1-1.5 tbsp (14-21g) brown sugar, 2 tbsp (30ml) balsamic vinegar, and ½ cup (125ml) unsalted chicken stock in Instant Pot. Carefully add in browned short ribs + meat juice.
  7. If you are making mashed potatoes: Cut 3 - 4 potatoes in half, then place them in a stainless steel bowl. Layer the bowl on top of the short ribs.
  8. Then, pressure cook:
  9. With Potatoes: High Pressure 42 minutes + Natural Release 15 minutes
  10. No Potatoes: High Pressure 45 minutes + Natural Release 15 minutes
  11. Optional - Make Mashed Potatoes: Drain & transfer the potatoes to a larger mixing bowl. Mash the potatoes with a potato masher. Mix in 100ml whole milk and 1 – 2 tbsp (14g -28g) unsalted butter. Season to taste with salt & pepper.
  12. Thicken & Seasonings: Carefully set aside the short ribs. Mix 2.5 tbsp (22.5g) cornstarch and 3 tbsp (45ml) cold water together in a small mixing bowl. Bring the sauce to a simmer and add the cornstarch mixture one third at a time to thicken the red wine sauce until desired thickness. Taste and season with more salt, black pepper, and brown sugar if necessary (For reference: we added 3 large pinches of salt).
  13. Serve Short Ribs: Carefully place the short ribs back in the sauce to keep them hot. Serve & be prepared for your guests to beg for more of this deliciously beautiful creation!
