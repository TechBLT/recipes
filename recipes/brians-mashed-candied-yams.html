Brians Mashed Candied Yams

Ingredients

  • 4-5
  • lbs garnet or jewel yams
  • 3
  • tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 1/2
  • tsp kosher salt
  • 8
  • tbsp salted butter
  • 3/4
  • cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2
  • cup heavy cream
  • 1/4
  • cup real maple syrup
  • 1
  • tsp vanilla extract
  • 1
  • tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2
  • tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4
  • tsp allspice
  • 1/4
  • tsp ground ginger
  • Optional: 1-2 tbsp bourbon
  • 4
  • tbsp cold butter, cubed
  • Mini marshmallows for topping

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 425F. Wash and dry yams, poke holes, rub with oil and salt.
  2. 2. Roast 70-90 minutes until very soft and syrupy. Let cool slightly and peel.
  3. 3. Mash yams in a large bowl until mostly smooth.
  4. 4. Make candy sauce: melt butter, whisk in brown sugar, add cream, maple syrup, spices, and vanilla (plus bourbon if using).
  5. 5. Pour sauce over yams and fold to combine.
  6. 6. Add 4 tbsp cold butter and 1/2 cup heavy cream. Whip until silky and thick.
  7. 7. Spread into baking dish and bake at 375F for 15 minutes to set the surface.
  8. 8. Top with mini marshmallows and bake 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
