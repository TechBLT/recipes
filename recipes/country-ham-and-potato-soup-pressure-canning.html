Country Ham & Potato Soup ~ Pressure Canning

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/D469A46A-CFDC-4837-9A34-683C7C215F26.jpg?resize=600%2C600&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • Yeilds 7 Qts
  • 14
  • Cups chicken broth
  • 14
  • Large potatoes, peeled and cubed in 1 inch pieces
  • 1 3/4
  • Cup chopped onions
  • 2 1/3
  • Cups ham, cubed small

Directions

  1. Prepare jars and canner.
  2. Fill jars halfway with potatoes, then add to each jar 1/4 cup onions, 1/3 cup ham.
  3. Fill jars to 1 inch with broth, de-bubble, wipe rims with vinegar, add lid and ring.
  4. Process quarts 90 minutes, pints 75 minutes at your pressure for your altitude.
  5. To serve: Heat up and add 1/4 cup heavy cream. Top with desired toppings such as sour cream, bacon bits, fresh herbs, cheese. Or change it up by adding broccoli and cheddar or cream cheese and chives……. Whatever you like!!
  6. Enjoy!
