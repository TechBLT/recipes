Garlic Butter Steak Bites with Roasted Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb sirloin steak, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2.5 cups broccoli florets
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1.25 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 1/4 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss broccoli with olive oil, salt and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes. Heat olive oil in skillet over high heat. Sear steak pieces 2–3 minutes until browned. Add garlic, butter, and seasonings and cook 1 minute. Serve with broccoli and lemon wedges.
