DIY Dishwasher Rinse Aid (Hard Water Safe)

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup distilled water
  • 1
  • tsp citric acid powder
  • Optional: 2–3 drops essential oil (lemon, orange, or lavender)

Directions

  1. 1) Warm distilled water slightly and stir in citric acid until fully dissolved.
  2. 2) Add essential oil if desired and mix.
  3. 3) Pour into a clean bottle.
  4. 4) Fill the dishwasher's rinse aid compartment with this mixture as needed.
