Bolognese Sauce For Canning Ala Brian

Ingredients

  • 10
  • lbs 85/15 ground beef
  • 10
  • mild Italian sausages, casings removed
  • 1
  • lb. Pancetta
  • 12
  • stalks celery, minced
  • 12
  • carrots, peeled and minced
  • 1
  • bag chopped onion, large bag Smart and Final (about 6 onions)
  • 24
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • Couple of bunches fresh basil, chopped
  • Couple of bunches fresh Italian parsley, chopped
  • 1
  • bottle red wine (I used cab)
  • Extra virgin Olive oil
  • 3
  • #10 cans crushed tomatoes in puree
  • Kosher salt and pepper to taste
  • Italian Seasoning from Smart and Final (grinder) to taste - I used quite a bit

Directions

  1. I wrote up how I made it…
  2. Brown meat
  3. Saute veggies in olive oil
  4. Add to 20 quart pot
  5. Add three cans of crushed tomatoes
  6. Add garlic
  7. Add wine
  8. Add chopped herbs
  9. Stir and simmer
  10. Taste and season with salt and pepper
  11. Crank that Italian seasoning for days…lol
  12. Process in jars - Quarts 90 minutes
