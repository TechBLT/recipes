Crispy Garlic-Herb Chicken Thighs with Lemon Yogurt Sauce (Serves 5)

Ingredients

  • Chicken:
  • 10
  • bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (5–6 lbs total)
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 2
  • tsp kosher salt
  • 1½
  • tsp black pepper
  • 2
  • tsp garlic powder
  • 2
  • tsp paprika
  • 2
  • tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½
  • tsp onion powder (optional)
  • Lemon Yogurt Sauce:
  • 1½
  • cups full-fat Greek yogurt
  • Juice of 1 large lemon
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1
  • small garlic clove, grated
  • 1–2
  • tbsp chopped fresh dill or parsley
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. 1. Pat chicken thighs very dry with paper towels.
  2. 2. Rub thighs with olive oil.
  3. 3. Mix salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, Italian seasoning, and onion powder. Coat chicken thoroughly.
  4. 4. Preheat air fryer to 390–400°F.
  5. 5. Air fry chicken for 24–28 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  6. 6. Cook to an internal temperature of 175–185°F for best texture.
  7. 7. For the sauce, mix Greek yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, grated garlic, herbs, and salt.
  8. 8. Refrigerate sauce until ready to serve.
  9. 9. Let chicken rest 5 minutes before serving. Spoon sauce over or serve on the side.
