Horseradish Sauce

Source: https://www.lifesambrosia.com/wp-content/uploads/166x250xHorseradish-Sauce-Recipe-Photo-8-562x848.jpg.pagespeed.ic.f_hvHMMuAf.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1/4
  • cup sour cream
  • 1
  • teaspoon prepared horseradish
  • 1
  • teaspoon Worcestershire
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon black pepper
  • salt

Directions

  1. Whisk all ingredients, except salt, together in a bowl. Season to taste with salt. Serve.
