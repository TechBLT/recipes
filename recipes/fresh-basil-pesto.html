Ingredients

  • 2
  • cups fresh basil leaves, packed (can sub half the basil leaves with baby spinach)
  • 1/2
  • cup freshly grated Romano or Parmesan cheese (about 2 ounces)
  • 1/2
  • cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3
  • cup pine nuts (can sub chopped walnuts)
  • 3
  • cloves garlic, minced (about 1 tablespoon)
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1/8
  • teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Pulse the basil and pine nuts:
  2. Place the basil leaves and pine nuts into the bowl of a food processor and pulse several times.
