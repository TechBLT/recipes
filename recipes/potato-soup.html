Potato Soup

Source: http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-U6KgDghgUDs/TZNSfMM9YWI/AAAAAAAAAO0/uLgZFSVcumg/s0/canning%2Bpencil%2Bsmall.jpg

Ingredients

  • 5 lb. red potatoes, peel & cubed
  • 6 ribs + the heart of celery, cubed
  • 3-4 lb. onions, cubed
  • 4 shredded carrots
  • 1 bouillon cube for each quart jar

Directions

  1. Layer in quart jar starting with potatoes (I fill half the jar with potatoes), fill with water leaving head space. Pressure can at 10 lbs. pressure for 40 minutes for quarts, 35 minutes for pints. TO MAKE SOUP: Put veggies in pan & heat (I mash my veggies just to lumpy). Add can milk & butter, salt & pepper to taste. I also have add Velveta cubed & melted in mixture before adding milk.
