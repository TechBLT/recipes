Taco Bell Taco Seasoning Clone/Copycat

Source: https://img.sndimg.com/food/image/upload/f_auto,c_thumb,q_73,ar_16:9,w_120/v1/img/recipes/10/59/93/piccBv06e.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1
  • tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1
  • tablespoon dried onion flakes
  • 1
  • teaspoon beef bouillon granules
  • 1
  • teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1
  • teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1
  • teaspoon paprika
  • 1
  • teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon onion salt
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon granulated sugar

Directions

  1. Combine and store in an air-tight container.
  2. Notes: To make taco meat, brown 1 lb ground beef in skillet; drain.
  3. Add seasoning mix and 3/4 cup of water.
  4. Bring to a boil then reduce heat.
  5. Simmer uncovered, 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
