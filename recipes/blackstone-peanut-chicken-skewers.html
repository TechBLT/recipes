Blackstone Peanut Chicken Skewers

Source: https://ifyougiveagirlagrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/IMG_0149.jpg

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsps rice vinegar
  • 2 Tbsps sriracha
  • 1 Tbsp garlic paste or minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp ginger paste or grated ginger
  • vegetable oil or cooking oil of choice
  • Optional: rice, yum yum sauce

Directions

  1. Trim the chicken thighs and cut into bite size pieces. Place the chicken in a gallon size plastic bag. Add the peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar, Sriracha, honey, garlic, and ginger to a bowl and whisk until evenly combined. Add half of this sauce to the bag with the chicken and massage until evenly combined, set the other half of the sauce aside for dipping. Marinate in the fridge several hours or overnight. Put the chicken on kabob skewers. Preheat the gridle to medium high heat. Add some oil once hot and cook the chicken skewers 4 to 5 minutes, flip and cook 4 to 5 more minutes. Serve the chicken skewers with the remaining peanut sauce and with rice or yum yum sauce if desired.
