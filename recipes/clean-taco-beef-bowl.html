Clean Taco Beef Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb ground beef
  • 1 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 5/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 5/8 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2.5 cups cauliflower rice
  • 1 1/4 avocados, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet. Add spices and cook 2–3 minutes. Prepare cauliflower rice. Serve beef over cauliflower rice. Top with avocado, tomatoes, cilantro, and lime wedges.
