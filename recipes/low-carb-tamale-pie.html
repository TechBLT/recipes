Low Carb Tamale Pie

Source: https://jenniferbanz.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/low-carb-tamale-pie.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2
  • lbs chuck roast
  • 1/2
  • cup water
  • 1
  • tsp chili powder
  • 1
  • tsp cumin
  • 1 1/2
  • tsp salt
  • 1/2
  • tsp black pepper
  • 1
  • can Enchilada Sauce See notes
  • 1
  • 4 oz can diced green chilis
  • 3
  • cups shredded cheddar cheese , divided
  • 4
  • eggs , beaten
  • 1
  • cup chicken broth
  • 1
  • cup Coconut flour
  • 1
  • tsp baking soda

Directions

  1. Cut the chuck roast into large chunks and season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper
  2. place beef and water in Instant Pot and set for 35 minutes, let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick release the rest
  3. Remove beef from instant pot and shred with 2 forks
  4. Alternatively, you can cook in the slow cooker for 8 hours on low or 4 hours on high.
  5. preheat oven to 400 degrees
  6. Combine shredded beef, enchilada sauce, and green chilis in a bowl
  7. pour into a rectangle casserole dish
  8. top with 2 cups of cheese
  9. To make the "cornbread" top: combine beaten eggs and chicken broth in a bowl
  10. In a separate bowl combine coconut flour and baking soda
  11. add the coconut flour mixture to the egg mixture
  12. add remaining cup of cheese to the batter. If it seems too thick, add more chicken broth. It should be easy to spread and not dry.
  13. spread over the top of the beef and cheese in casserole dish
  14. bake for 35-40 minutes until bubbly
  15. Serve with pico de gallo and sour cream
