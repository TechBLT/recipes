Chicken Tenderloins Parmesan-style

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs chicken tenderloins
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 zucchini (spiralized or sliced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. Season and pan-sear chicken until golden. Top with marinara, mozzarella, and parmesan. Bake or cover until cheese melts. Sauté zucchini noodles in olive oil until just tender. Serve chicken with zucchini noodles.
