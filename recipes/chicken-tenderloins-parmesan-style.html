Chicken Tenderloins Parmesan-style

Ingredients

  • 1-2
  • lbs chicken tenderloins
  • 2
  • cups marinara sauce
  • 1
  • cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4
  • cup grated parmesan
  • 2
  • zucchini (spiralized or sliced)
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. Season and pan-sear chicken until golden.
  2. Top with marinara, mozzarella, and parmesan.
  3. Bake or cover until cheese melts.
  4. Sauté zucchini noodles in olive oil until just tender.
  5. Serve chicken with zucchini noodles.
