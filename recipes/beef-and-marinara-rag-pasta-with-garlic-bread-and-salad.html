Beef & Marinara Ragù Pasta with Garlic Bread & Salad

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb ground beef
  • 1
  • jar marinara sauce (24 oz)
  • 1
  • lb pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 3
  • cloves garlic, minced (divided)
  • 1
  • medium onion, diced
  • 1
  • tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 4
  • slices sourdough bread (homemade)
  • 2
  • tbsp butter, softened
  • 2
  • cups salad greens
  • 2
  • tbsp Italian dressing (or your choice)

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add 2 cloves minced garlic and cook 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef, season with salt & pepper, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and oregano. Simmer 10–15 minutes on low heat.
  5. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  6. Combine pasta and sauce mixture in the skillet. Toss to coat.
  7. For garlic bread: Mix softened butter with 1 clove minced garlic. Spread over sourdough slices. Toast under broiler or on griddle until golden brown.
  8. For salad: Toss greens with dressing in a large bowl.
  9. Serve pasta with garlic bread and salad on the side.
