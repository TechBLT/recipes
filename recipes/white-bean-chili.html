White Bean Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 tbl olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tbl oregano
  • 2 tbl cumin
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 lbs ground turkey
  • 3 tbl flour
  • 2 cans of white beans, rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen corn, thawed
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Avocado

Directions

  1. Saute onions, chili powder, oregano, cumin and garlic with olive oil. Cook until onions are soft. Add ground turkey and brown. Add flour to thicken. Add beans, corn and chicken broth and stir to combine. Simmer for an hour or more.
