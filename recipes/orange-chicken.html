Orange Chicken

Source: http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_gx7HlpG6P7o/SYSFwssc2jI/AAAAAAAAAHA/PfOBgDOP_AA/s400/RDS_2729.JPG

Ingredients

  • Another clone of Panda Express.
  • 2
  • lbs chicken
  • 3/4
  • tsp ground ginger
  • 1
  • teaspoon garlic
  • 1
  • tablespoon rice wine
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 1/2
  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 1/2
  • tablespoons water
  • 5
  • tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 5
  • tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1
  • cup orange juice
  • zest of 1 orange
  • 3
  • pint jars

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces and stir fry
  2. Add all other ingredients in pan and bring to a light boil
  3. Pour into jars distributing evenly
  4. Add orange juice to fill line if needed
  5. For pints process 75 minutes at 11 lbs pressure
  6. Quarts would need 90 minutes.
  7. When serving add cornstarch to sauce to thicken.
  8. Serve over rice.
