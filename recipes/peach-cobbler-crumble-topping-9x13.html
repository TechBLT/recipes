Peach Cobbler – Crumble Topping (9x13)

Ingredients

  • 8
  • cups sliced peaches (fresh or frozen, peeled optional)
  • 1/2
  • cup flexible sweetener (sugar, allulose, or honey)
  • 2
  • tbsp cornstarch
  • 1
  • tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2
  • tsp nutmeg
  • 1
  • cup all-purpose flour (or gluten-free blend)
  • 1/2
  • cup rolled oats
  • 1/2
  • cup brown sugar or allulose brown sugar blend
  • 1/2
  • cup butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2
  • tsp salt

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. 2. Toss peaches with 1/2 cup sweetener, cornstarch, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Spread in a greased 9x13 baking dish.
  3. 3. In a bowl, combine flour, oats, brown sugar, and salt. Cut in butter with a fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. 4. Sprinkle crumble topping evenly over peaches.
  5. 5. Bake 40–45 minutes, until topping is golden and filling is bubbling.
  6. 6. Serve warm, optionally with ice cream or whipped cream.
