Squash Ravioli with Tomato Cream Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1
  • package squash ravioli
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 2
  • tbsp tomato paste
  • 1
  • cup cream or half-and-half
  • 2
  • zucchini
  • 2
  • cups cauliflower rice
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Cook ravioli per package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté tomato paste in olive oil, add cream, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss ravioli in sauce.
  4. Sauté zucchini ribbons until tender.
  5. Cook cauliflower rice in olive oil until softened, season with salt.
  6. Serve ravioli with zucchini ribbons and cauliflower rice on the side.
