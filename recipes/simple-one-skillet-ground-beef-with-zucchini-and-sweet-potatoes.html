Directions

  1. Heat the Oil:
  2. In a large non-stick skillet, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Cook the Onion and Garlic:
  4. Add 1 diced onion and sauté for 2–3 minutes until soft. Add 2 minced garlic cloves and stir for another 30 seconds.
  5. Add the Ground Beef:
  6. Add 1 lb ground beef to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula. Drain excess fat if needed.
  7. Season the Meat:
  8. Stir in ½ teaspoon each of smoked paprika, garlic powder, and oregano, along with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Add Sweet Potatoes:
  10. Add 2 diced sweet potatoes, cover, and let them cook with the beef for 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  11. Add Zucchini:
  12. Stir in 2 chopped zucchinis, cover again, and cook for another 6–8 minutes until veggies are tender.
  13. Finish and Serve:
  14. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro and top with cheese or avocado if desired.
  15. Dice sweet potatoes small so they cook evenly and quickly.
  16. Use a large non-stick or cast iron skillet to avoid overcrowding.
  17. Drain grease from the beef for a cleaner, lighter dish.
  18. Cover the skillet when cooking veggies to trap steam and soften them faster.
  19. Adjust seasoning to your liking—add chili flakes, cumin, or lemon juice.
  20. Swap ingredients as needed: try butternut squash instead of sweet potatoes, or bell peppers in place of zucchini.
  21. Build flavor by cooking beef with onion and garlic first.
  22. Start with sweet potatoes, since they take longer than zucchini.
  23. Add zucchini last and cook just until tender—avoid overcooking.
  24. Stir occasionally, not constantly, to keep veggies intact and get nice browning.
  25. Let the dish rest a minute off heat before serving to enhance flavor and texture.
