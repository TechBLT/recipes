Directions

  1. How To Hard Boil Eggs
  2. Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with water.
  3. Transfer to stovetop over high heat until water begins to boil.
  4. Boil for one minute, cover with lid, and remove from heat. Allow to sit for 17 minutes and then drain and transfer to an ice bath.
  5. Peel eggs and set aside.
  6. How To Make Deviled Eggs
  7. Slice eggs in half lengthwise.
  8. Remove yolks and transfer to a medium-sized bowl.
  9. Add mayo, butter, mustards, pickle juice, sugar, salt, pepper, and Tabasco sauce. Use a fork to mash well.
  10. Spoon filling into each egg white. Sprinkle with paprika and bacon (if using) and serve.
