Banana Pudding

Source: https://www.soulfullymade.com/banana-pudding/

Ingredients

  • 16 Oz Kraft Cool Whip
  • 2 – 5.1 oz Jello Instant Vanilla Pudding
  • 6 Cups of Milk
  • 3 Large Bananas
  • Chessmen Cookies 1 to 2 Bags or Nilla Wafers
  • 16 oz Cool Whip
  • 2 - 5.1 oz instant vanilla pudding
  • 6 cups milk
  • 3 large bananas, sliced
  • chessman cookies or vanilla wafers

Directions

  1. Mix 6 Cups of Milk with 2 Packages of Vanilla Pudding. Beat for 2 to 3 Minutes of level 2 to 3 of mixer. Fold in Cool Whip. Slice Bananas. Layer Pudding Mixture on bottom of Triffle Dish.Then place cookies and sliced bananas on top of pudding mixture. Then repeat. Usually makes 5 to 6 layers. Top with cookies or whipped cream if you would like to make it fancy! Mix 6 Cups of Milk with 2 Packages of Vanilla Pudding. Beat for 2 to 3 Minutes of level 2 to 3 of mixer. Fold in Cool Whip until combined. Layer Pudding Mixture on bottom of Dish. Then place cookies and sliced bananas on top of pudding mixture. Repeat to make 5 to 6 layers. Place in refrigerator for at least 2 hours to overnight until set and chilled. Top with cookies or whipped cream. (This can be done before or after chilling - cookies will be softer if done before and chilled overnight)
