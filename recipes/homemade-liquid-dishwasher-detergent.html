Homemade Liquid Dishwasher Detergent

Ingredients

  • 1 cup liquid castile soap
  • 1 cup distilled water
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. 1) In a large jar or bottle, combine distilled water and vinegar.
  2. Stir in the lemon juice.
  3. Add liquid castile soap last and gently swirl to mix (avoid too much foam).
  4. Store in a sealed container.
  5. Shake gently before each use.
  6. Use about 2 tablespoons per dishwasher load in the detergent compartment.
