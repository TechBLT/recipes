Teriyaki Salmon Bowls with Rice & Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 5 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 3 cups water or broth
  • 1 lb broccoli florets
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional garnish: sesame seeds, sliced green onions

Directions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker with water or broth until tender. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and teriyaki sauce. Let marinate 15 minutes if time allows. Bake salmon at 400°F for 12–15 minutes, or pan-sear until cooked through. Steam broccoli until crisp-tender. Season with salt and pepper. Assemble bowls: rice base + salmon + broccoli. Drizzle with extra teriyaki sauce and garnish if desired.
