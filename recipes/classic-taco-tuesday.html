Classic Taco Tuesday

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet or 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Taco shells or small tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Diced tomato
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Cooked Mexican rice or corn for serving

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain any excess fat. Add taco seasoning and water; simmer 5 minutes until thickened. Warm taco shells or tortillas. Assemble tacos with beef, cheese, lettuce, tomato, sour cream, and salsa. Serve with Mexican rice or corn on the side.
