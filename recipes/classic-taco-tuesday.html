Classic Taco Tuesday

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2
  • lb ground beef
  • 1
  • packet or 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1/2
  • cup water
  • Taco shells or small tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Diced tomato
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Cooked Mexican rice or corn for serving

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain any excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water; simmer 5 minutes until thickened.
  3. Warm taco shells or tortillas.
  4. Assemble tacos with beef, cheese, lettuce, tomato, sour cream, and salsa.
  5. Serve with Mexican rice or corn on the side.
