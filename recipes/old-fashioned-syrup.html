Old Fashioned Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup Demerara sugar (or Turbinado/raw sugar)
  • 1
  • cup water
  • 10–12
  • dashes Angostura bitters
  • 8
  • dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1 orange (avoid thick white pith)
  • Optional: 1 cinnamon stick, 2–3 cloves, 1 star anise

Directions

  1. Add Demerara sugar and water to a small saucepan over medium-low heat until dissolved.
  2. Add orange peel and any optional spices.
  3. Simmer gently for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters.
  6. Strain, bottle, and refrigerate up to 3–4 weeks.
