Old Fashioned Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Demerara sugar (or Turbinado/raw sugar)
  • 1 cup water
  • 10–12 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 8 dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1 orange (avoid thick white pith)
  • Optional: 1 cinnamon stick, 2–3 cloves, 1 star anise

Directions

  1. Add Demerara sugar and water to a small saucepan over medium-low heat until dissolved. Add orange peel and any optional spices. Simmer gently for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool for 10 minutes. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters. Strain, bottle, and refrigerate up to 3–4 weeks.
