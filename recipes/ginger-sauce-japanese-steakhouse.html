Ginger Sauce (Japanese Steakhouse)

Source: https://www.daringgourmet.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Ginger-Sauce-17-500x500.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1/2
  • cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 1/2
  • tablespoons chopped fresh ginger root , peeled (use more or less depending on strength preference)
  • 1/3
  • cup tamari (highly recommended for the best flavor, but can substitute low sodium soy sauce)
  • 3
  • tablespoons rice vinegar
  • zest and juice of one lemon (1 1/2-2 tablespoons juice)
  • 2
  • teaspoons brown sugar (optional)

Directions

  1. Place everything in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Store in a sealed jar in the fridge for up to 5 days.
