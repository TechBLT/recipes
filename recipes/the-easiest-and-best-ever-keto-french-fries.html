The Easiest & BEST Ever Keto French Fries

Source: https://www.ketofocus.com/wp-content/uploads/almond-flour-keto-french-fries-1.jpg

Ingredients

  • Low Carb Keto French Fries Ingredients
  • 100
  • g almond flour (1/2 cup + 1/3 cup)
  • 1
  • tablespoon xanthan gum
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon salt
  • 6
  • tablespoons hot water
  • 20
  • drops mashed potato flavor drops, optional
  • olive oil for frying

Directions

  1. Whisk ingredients
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together almond flour, xanthan gum and salt.
  3. Mix & knead
  4. Mix in hot water and flavor extract if using. Knead together with hands until combined.
  5. Flatten the dough
  6. Place dough in between two sheets of parchment paper and roll out to an 1/4 inch thick using a rolling pin. Slice dough into small strips. Try to get them as thin as possible, around 1/8 to 1/4 inch thick.
  7. If you want to freeze
  8. Lay strips of dough onto a parchment lined baking tray and stick in the freezer. Once frozen, remove the fries from the tray and store in freezer safe bag in the freezer. Proceed with directions below to cook from frozen. img: almond-flour-keto-french-fries-step-4.jpg
  9. Frying instructions
  10. To a large skillet, add enough olive oil to cover the keto french fries as they cook. Let heat over medium heat. Once the oil is heated, add a single layer of fries to the oil and let them cook until golden brown on all sides. May need to turn them to cook all sides. Remove from the oil and onto a paper towel lined plate. Sprinkle with additional salt if needed.
  11. Baking instructions
  12. To bake, spread french fries out in a single layer onto a baking tray. Spray the fries with olive oil. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes
  13. Air fryer instructions
  14. To air fry, spread french fries onto the air fryer basket or tray in a single layer. Spray the fries with olive oil. Air fry at 400 degrees for 6 minutes.
