Easy Sofrito – Jeni Gough

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/73B19A78-A17E-4F2E-91FF-33C02C2FC212.jpg?fit=594%2C640&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches Fresh cilantro, washed well
  • 2 Large onions, quartered
  • 1 – 2 heads Garlic, peeled
  • 1 Red bell pepper, seeded and diced large
  • 1 Green or orange bell pepper, seeded and diced large
  • 2 Cubano peppers, seeded and diced large

Directions

  1. Toss it all in a blender for puréed consistency or food processor for chunkier consistency. Freeze in ice cube trays, then transfer to a freezer bag. Will keep in your freezer for up to 6 months.
