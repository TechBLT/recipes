Broccoli and Cheese Chaffle

Source: https://www.midgetmomma.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Broccoli-and-cheese-chaffle-31-552x800.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped broccoli
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond flour

Directions

  1. In a bowl mix almond flour, cheddar cheese, egg and garlic powder. I find it easiest to mix everything using a fork. Add half the Broccoli and Cheese Chaffle batter to the Dish Mini waffle maker at a time. Cook chaffle batter in the waffle maker for 4 minutes. Let each chaffle sit for 1-2 minutes on a plate to firm up. Enjoy alone or dipping in sour cream or ranch dressing.
