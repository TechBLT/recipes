Riesling Pickled Radishes

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/CE97AD8F-F9D2-4724-8504-F0E50C1D9BAB.jpg?resize=600%2C600&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 3
  • cups Water
  • 2
  • lbs Radishes
  • 2
  • tbsp Kosher salt
  • 1
  • cup White Vinegar
  • 1 3/4
  • cup Riesling or other sweet white wine
  • 1
  • tbsp Tricolor peppercorns
  • 1
  • tsp Whole cloves
  • 1/4
  • cup Granulated sugar

Directions

  1. Wash, pare & slice radishes 1/4 inch thickness. Place into a bowl, sprinkle with salt and pour 3 cups of water over top. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours.
  2. Wash jars, lids, rings in hot, soapy water. Fill bath canner with water, add jars and bring to a simmer 20 minutes.
  3. In a pot, bring to a boil, wine, vinegar, sugar and spices. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Pack jars with radishes, pour over hot brine leaving 1/4 inch headspace, de-bubble, wipe rims. place lids and rings to fingertip tight, add to simmering water bath canner.
  5. process jars 10 minutes adjusting time by 5 minutes for your altitude.
