Green Salad with Fresh Herbs and Red Wine Vinaigrette

Source: https://www.simplyrecipes.com/thmb/QoYfWAB1HaPZSWM8SDPYEHJlKAs=/750x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc()/Simply-Recipes-Salad-Fresh-Herbs-LEAD-05-9741f0ac52ec4689bd4fed5a845032de.jpg

Ingredients

  • For the salad:
  • 1 large head butter lettuce (about 9 inches wide at crown)
  • 1/2 cup lightly packed flat-leaf parsley leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 bunch chives (about 35 thin chives), cut crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 8 large mint leaves, very thinly sliced
  • For the dressing:
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Tear the lettuce: Tear the lettuce into large pieces, about 2 to 3 inches long (you’ll have about 4 to 5 cups, lightly packed.) Simply Recipes / Lori Rice Put the lettuce and herbs in a large salad bowl; set aside. Make the salad dressing: In a jar with a tight lid, combine the olive oil, vinegar, mustard, water, salt, and pepper. Close the jar firmly, and shake the dressing until it is thoroughly combined, about 10 seconds. Simply Recipes / Lori Rice Simply Recipes / Lori Rice Dress the salad: Dress the salad with about 2/3 of the dressing, and gently toss the salad.
