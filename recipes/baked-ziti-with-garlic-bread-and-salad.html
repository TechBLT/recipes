Baked Ziti with Garlic Bread and Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ziti pasta
  • 1 lb Italian sausage (mild or spicy)
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 loaf French bread
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cucumber slices
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Italian vinaigrette

Directions

  1. 1. Cook pasta until just al dente. Drain.
  2. Brown sausage in skillet. Stir in marinara.
  3. In baking dish, layer pasta, ricotta, sausage sauce, mozzarella. Repeat layers. Top with Parmesan.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 25–30 min until bubbly and golden.
  5. For garlic bread: Mix butter, garlic, and parsley. Spread on bread halves. Toast until golden.
  6. Toss salad greens, cucumber, and cherry tomatoes with vinaigrette.
  7. Serve baked ziti with garlic bread and salad.
