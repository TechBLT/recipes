Top Sirloin with Mushroom-Onion Pan Sauce, Mashed Potatoes & Salad

Ingredients

  • 2
  • lbs top sirloin steak
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1
  • onion, thinly sliced
  • 8
  • oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1
  • cup beef broth
  • 1
  • tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2
  • tbsp butter
  • 2
  • lbs potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2
  • cup milk
  • 2
  • tbsp butter (for potatoes)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2
  • cups salad greens
  • 2
  • tbsp dressing of choice

Directions

  1. Sous vide sirloin at 132°F for 2 hours. Remove, pat dry.
  2. Heat skillet with olive oil. Sear steak 1–2 minutes per side until browned. Remove and rest.
  3. In the same skillet, add onions and mushrooms. Cook until softened and golden.
  4. Add garlic, cook 1 minute. Stir in beef broth and Worcestershire. Simmer until slightly reduced.
  5. Whisk in butter to finish sauce. Slice steak and serve topped with sauce.
  6. For potatoes: Boil potatoes until tender, drain. Mash with milk, butter, salt, and pepper.
  7. Toss salad greens with dressing.
  8. Serve steak with mashed potatoes and salad.
