Sous Vide New York Strip Roast Beef (Deli-Style)

Ingredients

  • 1
  • whole New York strip (about 7–10 lbs, or cut in half to fit bag)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1
  • tsp garlic powder
  • 1
  • tsp onion powder
  • 1
  • tsp smoked paprika (optional)
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary sprigs (optional)
  • 1
  • tbsp avocado oil (for searing)

Directions

  1. 1. Trim excess fat cap from New York strip, leaving about 1/4 inch for flavor.
  2. 2. Season generously with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and optional paprika. Add fresh thyme or rosemary if desired.
  3. 3. Place roast in a vacuum-seal or freezer bag (using water displacement method if needed).
  4. 4. Sous vide at 134°F (57°C) for medium-rare or 138°F (59°C) for medium, for 6–8 hours.
  5. 5. Remove from bag, discard liquid, and pat roast completely dry.
  6. 6. Heat cast iron skillet or grill to high heat. Sear roast quickly, 30–45 seconds per side, until browned.
  7. 7. Rest briefly, then chill in refrigerator before slicing thin for deli-style sandwiches.
  8. 8. Slice across the grain as thin as possible. Serve on bread with horseradish cream, au jus, or your favorite toppings.
