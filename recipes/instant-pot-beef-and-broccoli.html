Instant Pot Beef and Broccoli

Source: https://realbalanced.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/instant-pot-beef-broccoli-in-a-bowl-on-top-of-a-marble-counter.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ lbs boneless chuck roast
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • ½ cup liquid aminos
  • ¼ cup classic monk fruit sweetener (use code “REALBALANCED” for 20% off Lakanto products)
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 12 oz raw broccoli florets
  • 1 ¼ tsp xanthan gum

Directions

  1. Cutting against the grain, slice chuck roasts into thin strips. To a mixing bowl, add beef broth, liquid aminos, classic monk fruit sweetener, and ground ginger. Whisk together until well-incorporated. Add sliced beef strips and stir to coat beef in sauce. Set aside. Turn Instant Pot to Saute setting, add avocado oil, and, once hot, add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add beef and sauce to Instant Pot and screw on Instant Pot lid. Set Instant Pot to Manual setting for 15 minutes. While beef is cooking, steam broccoli in a covered steamer basket over medium-high heat until just barely tender, about 8-10 minutes. (Alternatively, to steam broccoli, in a large microwave-safe bowl, add broccoli florets and 1 cup of water. Microwave until broccoli is just barely tender, about 4-5 minutes. Drain excess water from microwaved steamed broccoli.) Manually release steam once Instant Pot timer has gone off. Remove lid and, using a ladle, scoop out a ¾ cup of the sauce and transfer to a mixing bowl. In ¼ tsp increments, add xanthan gum and whisk vigorously until sauce thickens. Pour the thickened sauce back into Instant Pot. Add steamed broccoli to beef to Instant Pot insert. Gently fold in the broccoli to the beef and sauce mixture. Serve hot on its own or with a side of cauliflower rice.
