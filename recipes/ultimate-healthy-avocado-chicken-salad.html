Ultimate Healthy Avocado Chicken Salad

Source: https://fromscratchfast.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/Avocado-Chicken-Salad-Recipe-11.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe California Avocados, divided
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cups shredded rotisserie chicken (from 1 chicken)
  • ½ red bell pepper, finely diced
  • ½ jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely diced
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped cilantro

Directions

  1. Cut one avocado in half, remove and discard the pit, then scrape the flesh into a large bowl. Add the lime juice. Using a fork, mash the avocado until smooth. Add the olive oil and granulated garlic, and season with salt and pepper. Stir to combine. Add the chicken, red pepper, jalapeño, scallions and cilantro. Stir to combine, and season with more salt and pepper to taste. Cut the remaining avocado in half, remove the pit, then cut it into slices. Serve the chicken salad with the avocado slices in sandwiches, wraps and salads!
