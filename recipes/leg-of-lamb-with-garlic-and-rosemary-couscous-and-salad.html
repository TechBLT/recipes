Leg of Lamb with Garlic & Rosemary, Couscous & Salad

Ingredients

  • 1
  • leg of lamb (4–5 lbs)
  • 3
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 4
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 2
  • tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1
  • tsp salt
  • 1/2
  • tsp black pepper
  • 2
  • cups couscous
  • 2 1/4
  • cups chicken broth
  • 2
  • cups salad greens
  • 2
  • tbsp dressing of choice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Rub lamb with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  2. Roast in oven for 1.5–2 hours, until internal temp reaches 135°F for medium-rare or 145°F for medium. Rest 15 minutes before slicing.
  3. For couscous: Bring chicken broth to a boil. Stir in couscous, cover, and remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes, then fluff with fork.
  4. Toss salad greens with dressing.
  5. Serve sliced lamb with couscous and salad.
