Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry (Blackstone Griddle Style)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs flank steak (or sirloin), thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (or honey)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar (optional)
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated (or 1/2 tsp ground ginger)
  • 4 cups broccoli florets (fresh or blanched if preferred softer)
  • 1 small onion, sliced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil (vegetable, canola, or avocado oil)
  • 2-3 tbsp stir-fry sauce or reserved marinade
  • Sesame seeds & green onions for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. 1. Combine sliced beef with marinade ingredients. Let sit for 15–30 minutes.
  2. Heat griddle to medium-high and oil surface.
  3. Sear beef 1–2 minutes per side, then set aside.
  4. Stir-fry broccoli (and onions) for 3–4 minutes until crisp-tender.
  5. Return beef, toss with sauce, and cook 1 more minute.
  6. Serve hot over rice or noodles. Garnish if desired.
