Healthy BBQ Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 3 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2–3 Tbsp Allulose (to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. 1. In a saucepan, combine tomato sauce, vinegar, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard.
  2. Stir in smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, and Allulose.
  3. Simmer gently over medium-low heat for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
  4. Taste and adjust: add more Allulose if too tangy, or a splash more vinegar if too sweet. Add extra Worcestershire for savory depth.
  5. Cool slightly before using. Brush onto ribs during the last 30 minutes of smoking or grilling, and serve extra sauce on the side.
