Extra Moisturizing Foaming Hand Soap

Ingredients

  • 2
  • tbsp liquid castile soap (unscented or gentle)
  • 1–1.25
  • cups distilled or boiled & cooled water
  • 2
  • tsp sweet almond oil or jojoba oil (extra moisture)
  • 1
  • tsp vegetable glycerin (optional, for added hydration)
  • 8–12
  • drops lavender or chamomile essential oil (gentle fragrance)

Directions

  1. 1. Add the liquid castile soap to a clean foaming soap dispenser.
  2. 2. Add the sweet almond oil or jojoba oil.
  3. 3. Stir in the vegetable glycerin if using.
  4. 4. Add the essential oil drops for fragrance.
  5. 5. Slowly fill the rest of the dispenser with distilled water, leaving a little room at the top.
  6. 6. Screw on the pump and gently shake to combine.
  7. 7. Use as you would regular foaming soap.
