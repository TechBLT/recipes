Blue Ribbon Canned French Onion Soup

Source: https://ruralhighness.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/img_0079-768x1024.jpg

Ingredients

  • 6
  • pounds onions
  • Butter
  • 16
  • cups beef stock
  • 1
  • cup dry red wine
  • 1
  • Tbsp parsley
  • 1/2
  • Tbsp granulated garlic
  • 1/4
  • tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4
  • tsp black pepper
  • 2
  • bay leaves

Directions

  1. Slice onions.
  2. Sweat onions in butter. Cook until translucent and a little tender.
  3. Pour beef stock, wine, and spices into a stock pot and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Prepare 6 quart jars and lids for canning.
  5. Fill jars half full with onions.
  6. Pour broth into jars, leaving 1 inch headspace. Top with lids and rings.
  7. Process in pressure canner for 75 minutes at 11 pounds of pressure. (Check your pressure canning handbook for altitude adjustments.)
  8. Serve topped with croutons and melted mozzarella cheese.
