14 Carrot Cake (No Nuts)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2
  • cups oil
  • 2
  • cups sugar
  • 4
  • eggs
  • 2
  • cups grated carrots
  • 1
  • cup crushed pineapple with juice
  • 2 1/2
  • cups flour
  • 1
  • tsp baking soda
  • 1
  • tsp baking powder
  • 1/2
  • tsp salt
  • 2
  • tsp cinnamon
  • Frosting:
  • 3
  • cups powdered sugar
  • 2
  • tsp vanilla
  • 1/4
  • cup butter
  • 8
  • oz cream cheese

Directions

  1. Beat oil, sugar, and eggs. Add carrots and pineapple; mix well. Add dry ingredients lightly, beating as you blend.
  2. Pour into a 13x9 inch pan. Bake at 350°F for 30–40 minutes.
  3. Frosting: Cream butter and cream cheese together. Gradually add sugar and beat until fluffy. Add vanilla and mix well.
