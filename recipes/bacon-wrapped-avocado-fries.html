Bacon-Wrapped Avocado Fries

Source: https://blog.paleohacks.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/SCHEMA-PHOTO-Bacon-Wrapped-Avocado-Fries.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 20 strips of pasture-raised bacon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425ºF and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Remove the pits from the avocados and slice the avocado into thin strips lengthwise (5 per half). Wrap each avocado slice with one strip of bacon, and place on the baking sheet. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the bacon is crisp. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.
