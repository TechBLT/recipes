Ground Beef Quesadillas with Mexican Rice & Beans

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb ground beef
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1
  • onion, sliced (optional, for adults)
  • 1
  • bell pepper, sliced (optional, for adults)
  • 1
  • tsp chili powder
  • 1
  • tsp cumin
  • 1
  • tsp garlic powder
  • 8
  • flour tortillas
  • 2
  • cups shredded cheese
  • 1
  • can beans (black or pinto), warmed
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in skillet. Add ground beef, season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned.
  2. Optional: In a separate skillet, sauté onions and peppers until softened and lightly charred (for adults).
  3. Heat griddle. Place tortilla, add cheese, ground beef, and optional peppers/onions. Top with another tortilla. Cook until cheese melts and tortillas are golden brown on both sides.
  4. For kids: Make plain cheese + beef quesadillas without peppers/onions.
  5. Slice quesadillas into wedges. Serve with beans and Mexican Rice.
