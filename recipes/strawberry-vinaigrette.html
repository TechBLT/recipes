Strawberry Vinaigrette

Source: https://lilluna.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/strawberry-vinaigertte-resize-5-560x560.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • X 2X 3X
  • 1
  • cup strawberries with tops cut off
  • 2
  • tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. Make the dressing by blending all the ingredients in a blender or food processor.
  2. Refrigerate dressing until ready to serve. Add dressing to the salad right before serving.
