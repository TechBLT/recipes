White Bread Keto Chaffle

Source: https://i0.wp.com/www.buttertogetherkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/white-bread-keto-chaffle2.jpeg?resize=200%2C300&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Almond Flour
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Water

Directions

  1. Preheat mini waffle maker. In a bowl, whisk the egg until beaten. Add almond flour, mayonnaise, baking powder, and water. When the waffle maker is heated, carefully pour 1/2 of the batter in the waffle maker and close the top. Allow to cook for 3-5 minutes. Carefully remove from the waffle maker and set aside for 2-3 minutes to crisp up. Repeat instructions again for the second chaffle.
