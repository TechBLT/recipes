Low Carb Moscow Mule (Sugar Free)

Source: https://www.ibreatheimhungry.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/Mules2WMsmall-500x686.jpg

Ingredients

  • For the Ginger Syrup:
  • 1/2
  • cup thinly sliced, peeled ginger
  • 2
  • cups water
  • 1/3
  • cup granulated sugar substitute (I used Swerve)
  • For the Moscow Mule:
  • 4
  • oz premium vodka
  • 1
  • oz fresh lime juice
  • 1
  • oz ginger syrup (recipe above)
  • 8
  • oz diet ginger ale
  • fresh mint leaves to garnish

Directions

  1. For the Ginger Syrup:
  2. Combine the ginger, water and sweetener in a small saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat. Lower the heat to medium and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cool for 1 hour, then strain and store in a clean jar in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
  5. For the Moscow Mules:
  6. Combine the vodka, lime juice, ginger syrup and ginger ale in a small pitcher. Stir well.
  7. Pour over ice and garnish with fresh mint. For a stronger mint flavor, muddle (smash) the mint leaves around in the bottom of the mug slightly before drinking.
