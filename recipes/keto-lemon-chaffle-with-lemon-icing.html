Keto Lemon Chaffle with Lemon Icing

Source: https://www.ketofocus.com/assets/img/recipes/keto-lemon-chaffle-step-2.jpg

Ingredients

  • Keto Lemon Chaffle with Lemon Icing Ingredients
  • 1
  • egg
  • 1
  • oz cream cheese
  • 2
  • tablespoons almond flour
  • 1
  • tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2
  • teaspoons monk fruit
  • ½
  • teaspoon lemon zest
  • ¼
  • teaspoon baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 2
  • tablespoons powdered erythritol
  • 4
  • teaspoons heavy cream
  • 1
  • teaspoon lemon juice
  • dash lemon zest

Directions

  1. Keto Lemon Chaffle with Lemon Icing Directions
  2. 1 Preheat waffle maker to medium high heat.
  3. 2 Whisk together the chaffle ingredients - egg, cream cheese, almond flour, lemon juice, monk fruit, lemon zest, baking powder and salt.
  4. 3 Pour lemon chaffle batter into the center of the waffle iron. Close the waffle maker and let cook for 3-5 minutes or until waffle is golden brown and set. If using a mini waffle maker, only pour in half the batter.
  5. 4 Remove chaffle from the waffle maker.
  6. 5 Whisk together lemon icing ingredients - powdered erythritol, heavy cream, lemon juice and lemon zest. Pour over lemon chaffle.
