Sous Vide Ball Tip Roast Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 ball tip roast (2–3 lb)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter for searing
  • Fresh herbs (optional: rosemary or thyme)
  • Roasted Brussels sprouts
  • Baked or smashed potatoes for serving

Directions

  1. Season roast generously with salt, pepper, and herbs. Vacuum seal or bag using water displacement method. Sous vide at 133°F for 8–10 hours for medium-rare. Remove from bag, pat dry, and sear in hot skillet with oil or butter 1–2 min per side. Slice thinly across the grain and serve with sides.
