Pork Pozole Verde (canning)

Source: https://thefrayedapron.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/pork-pozole-verde.jpg

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds pork shoulder trim fat and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • 8 cups water w/ bouillon
  • 1/4 #10 crushed tomatillos
  • 1 large white onion chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic peeled
  • 1 Jalapeno pepper chopped
  • 1 Anaheim peppers chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 Tbsp dried Mexican oregano
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 large handful fresh cilantro stems and leaves
  • 8 cups canned white hominy drained and rinsed

Directions

  1. Roast pork in a single layer on baking sheets w/ parchment paper @ 425 for 30 minutes stirring halfway. Sprinkle with kosher salt and pepper. Add water to pot Add bouillon Add tomatillos to broth Add spices Add bay leaf Bring to boiling Divide roasted pork between jars Divide chopped onions Smash one clove garlic and add to each jar Add 1 cup hominy per jar Top off with tomatillo/broth to 1 1/2 headspace Add two turns crushed salt per jar Add two turns crushed pepper per jar Wipe jar rims with vinegar and install lids hand tight Process for 90 minutes When serving: Garnish with sliced radish and cabbage with a sprinkle of chopped cilantro. Serve with warm tortillas and fresh lime wedges.
