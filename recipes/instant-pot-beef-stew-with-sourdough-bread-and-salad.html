Instant Pot Beef Stew with Sourdough Bread & Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chuck roast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 tbsp flour (optional, for thickening)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Sourdough bread, sliced
  • 2 cups salad greens
  • 2 tbsp dressing of choice

Directions

  1. Set Instant Pot to sauté. Heat olive oil, brown beef cubes in batches. Remove and set aside. Add onion, carrots, and garlic. Sauté until softened. Stir in tomato paste, thyme, and rosemary. Cook 1 minute. Return beef to pot. Add potatoes and beef broth. Season with salt and pepper. Pressure cook on high for 35 minutes. Allow natural release for 10 minutes. Optional: Stir in flour slurry (flour + water) to thicken stew. Simmer a few minutes on sauté. Slice sourdough bread and toast if desired. Toss salad greens with dressing. Serve stew hot with sourdough bread and salad on the side.
