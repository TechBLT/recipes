Chicken Parmesan Tenderloins with Pasta, Salad & Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken tenderloins
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 lb pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for pasta)
  • 2 cups salad greens
  • 2 tbsp dressing of choice
  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Sous vide chicken tenderloins at 138°F for 1 hour (same day cooking). If storing in fridge, rewarm in sous vide for 15 minutes before finishing. Pat chicken dry. Dredge in flour, dip in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs. Place breaded chicken on baking sheet. Bake at 425°F for 8–10 minutes until golden. Top each with marinara sauce and mozzarella. Bake another 3–5 minutes until cheese is melted. Meanwhile, cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain, toss with olive oil, keep warm. For garlic bread: Mix softened butter with garlic. Spread over sourdough slices. Toast under broiler or on griddle until golden. Toss salad greens with dressing. Serve chicken parm with pasta, garlic bread, and salad.
