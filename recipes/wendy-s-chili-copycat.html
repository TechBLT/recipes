Wendy’s Chili (Copycat)

Source: https://dinnerthendessert.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/Wendys-Chili-2-500x750.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2
  • lbs ground beef 85/15
  • 29
  • ounces canned tomato sauce
  • 29
  • ounces canned kidney beans not drained
  • 29
  • ounces canned pinto beans not drained
  • 28
  • ounces canned tomatoes chopped (not drained)
  • 1
  • yellow onion diced
  • 1
  • diced jalapeno de-seeded and de-veined
  • 2
  • stalks celery chopped
  • 1
  • green bell pepper chopped
  • 1
  • tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1/4
  • cup chili powder
  • 1
  • teaspoon black pepper
  • 2
  • teaspoons salt
  • 1
  • cup water
  • 1
  • tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. In a heavy bottomed stockpot brown the ground beef.
  2. Drain the fat.
  3. Add in the rest of the ingredients (except the butter) and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower to a simmer and cook uncovered (please keep at a simmer level) for 2 hours stirring occasionally.
  5. Add in the butter and stir until fully melted and incorporated.
  6. Serve.
