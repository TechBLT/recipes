Perfect Italian Meat Sauce

Source: https://keepingitsimpleitalian.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/03/Italian-meat-sauce.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb Ground beef I prefer 85% lean ground beef.
  • 28
  • ounces San Marzano tomatoes crushed
  • ½
  • medium yellow onion diced very fine
  • 2
  • cloves garlic diced very fine
  • 1 ½
  • tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • ⅛
  • teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2
  • fresh basil leaves
  • ⅛
  • teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1
  • teaspoon kosher salt plus more to taste
  • sugar optional: a pinch at a time if your sauce tastes too acidic
  • 1 ½
  • tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • ½
  • medium yellow onion diced very fine,
  • ⅛
  • teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1
  • lb Ground beef
  • 2
  • fresh basil leaves,
  • 1
  • teaspoon kosher salt,
  • ⅛
  • teaspoon dried oregano

Directions

  1. Heat a large sauté pan over medium heat.
  2. Add your olive oil.
  3. Now add your red pepper flakes, onion and a pinch of salt and cook until the onions are translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove onions from pot on to a plate and set aside.
  5. In the same pot, add your ground beef and brown on all sides making sure to break up the meat as much as possible while it cooks (about 5 minutes).
  6. Add the onions back to the pot and mix in to the ground beef.
  7. Now add your garlic and cook for one more minute.
  8. 2 cloves garlic diced very fine
  9. Add your tomatoes to the pot.
  10. 28 ounces San Marzano tomatoes crushed
  11. Add your salt, oregano and basil and stir.
  12. Bring to a simmer then reduce heat to low and let simmer uncovered for 15 minutes.
  13. Taste the sauce for any additional salt.
  14. Serve with your favorite pasta and enjoy!
