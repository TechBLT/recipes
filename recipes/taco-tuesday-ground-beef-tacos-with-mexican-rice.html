Taco Tuesday – Ground Beef Tacos with Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb ground beef
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1
  • onion, diced
  • 3
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1
  • packet taco seasoning (or homemade blend)
  • 1/2
  • cup tomato sauce
  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa or guacamole
  • 2
  • cups long grain rice
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2
  • onion, diced
  • 3
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4
  • cup tomato paste
  • 3
  • cups chicken broth
  • 1
  • tsp cumin
  • 1
  • tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. 1. Heat olive oil in a skillet. Cook onion and garlic until softened. Add ground beef and brown.
  2. 2. Stir in taco seasoning and tomato sauce. Simmer 5 minutes.
  3. 3. Warm tortillas. Assemble tacos with lettuce, tomato, cheese, sour cream, salsa, or guacamole.
  4. 4. For rice: Heat oil in saucepan. Add onion and garlic, cook 2 min.
  5. 5. Stir in rice and toast lightly. Add tomato paste, cumin, chili powder, chicken broth, and salt.
  6. 6. Bring to boil, cover, reduce heat to low. Cook 18–20 minutes until rice is fluffy.
  7. 7. Serve tacos with Mexican rice on the side.
