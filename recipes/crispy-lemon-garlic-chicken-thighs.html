Crispy Lemon Garlic Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 5 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Juice of 1 1/4 lemons
  • 2.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 1/4 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano. Sear chicken skin-side down until crispy. Flip chicken, add garlic and lemon juice. Bake 25–30 minutes until fully cooked and internal temperature reaches 165°F.
