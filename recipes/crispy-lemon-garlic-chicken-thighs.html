Crispy Lemon Garlic Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 8
  • bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 5
  • cloves garlic, smashed
  • Juice of 1 1/4 lemons
  • 2.5
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/4
  • tsp paprika
  • 1 1/4
  • tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  3. Sear chicken skin-side down until crispy.
  4. Flip chicken, add garlic and lemon juice.
  5. Bake 25–30 minutes until fully cooked and internal temperature reaches 165°F.
