The Best Easy Italian Meat Sauce

Source: https://www.jerseygirlcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/meat-sauce-on-pasta-150x217.jpg

Ingredients

  • 12
  • ounces Italian sausage (bulk or casings removed)
  • 16
  • ounces ground beef (85% lean)
  • 2
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • ½
  • cup chopped onion
  • 4
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 2
  • cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1
  • tablespoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1
  • tablespoon dried basil
  • 2
  • tablespoons tomato paste
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Saute sausage and ground beef on medium high heat in a large pot or dutch oven until no longer pink. Remove or drain the extra grease.
  2. Add the olive oil, onion and garlic and cook for a few minutes until softened. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir well. Bring to a simmer and reduce heat to medium- low. Add Italian seasoning, dried basil and tomato paste. Mix well.
  3. Cover the pot and simmer for 20 minutes. Taste and season with salt and pepper. Cook for another 15 minutes uncovered or until sauce is to desired thickness.
  4. Stir in some fresh herbs if desired and serve with pasta.
