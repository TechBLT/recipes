Cheeseburger Soup

Source: https://thefeatherednester.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/IMG_3141-735x551.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb ground beef
  • 1
  • medium onion, chopped
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 2
  • cans (10 oz) Rotel Mild diced tomatoes, undrained (see FAQ for substitutions)
  • 1
  • lb Velveeta Cheese, cut into cubes (see FAQ for substitutions)
  • salt & ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Stove Top Instructions
  2. In a medium dutch oven or soup pot, brown the beef, onions, and garlic; drain fat off.
  3. Add Rotel and bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Add a lid to the pot, and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Turn off heat and stir in Velveeta until it melts. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Garnish with crushed corn chips, cheese, or even bacon bits. Leftovers can be frozen for up to 3 months.
  5. Slow Cooker Instructions
  6. Brown the beef, onions, and garlic in a skillet. Drain fat and add to the crockpot. Add the rest of the ingredients, and cook on low for 5-7 hours. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
  7. Garnish with crushed corn chips, cheese, or even bacon bits. Leftovers can be frozen for up to 3 months.
