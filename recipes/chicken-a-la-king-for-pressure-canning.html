Chicken a La King for Pressure Canning

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/chicken-a-la-king.jpg?resize=600%2C750&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 5
  • lbs Chicken, white or dark meat cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1
  • Large onion, diced
  • 1
  • lb Mushrooms, cleaned and sliced or chunked
  • 12
  • oz (3 4oz jars) Diced pimientos
  • 18
  • Cups Chicken broth
  • 1 1/2
  • tsp Onion powder
  • 4
  • cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 3/4
  • tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 2
  • tsp dried parsley

Directions

  1. Wash jars, lids and rings in hot soapy water. Prepare pressure canner and fill with water following canner instructions. Bring canner to a low simmer.
  2. Add chicken broth to pot along with garlic, onion powder, pepper and parsley. Bring to a boil and simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Divide chicken up evenly in jars. To each jar add: 1/3 cup onions, 2/3 cup mushrooms and 1 rounded tablespoon pimientos.
  4. Fill jars with broth leaving 1 inch headspace. Wipe rims, place on lids and rings to fingertip tight. Place jars in simmering canner.
  5. Process quarts 90 minutes and pints 75 minutes.
