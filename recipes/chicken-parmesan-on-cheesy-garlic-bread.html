Chicken Parmesan on Cheesy Garlic Bread

Source: https://www.shugarysweets.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/chicken-parmesan-garlic-bread-2-480x480.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1 box frozen On-Cor Breaded Chicken Parmagiana with Tomato Sauce
  • 1 loaf Italian Bread (about 14 oz size)
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 Tbsp shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1 Tbsp fresh parsley chopped

Directions

  1. Cook On-Cor according to package directions. Will bake in a 375 degree F oven for about 55-60 minutes. While entrée is cooking, prep the cheesy garlic bread. Slice the bread (end to end) in half. Cut two large sheets of foil to overlap a large baking sheet (it will be used to wrap around the bread). Place bread, cut side up on the foil lined baking sheet. In a small bowl, mix the butter, garlic, salt, and pepper together. Spread evenly over the cut side of bread, all the way to the edges. Wrap foil over bread loosely, pinching the edges together. Remove entree from oven when it's finished, and slide bread into oven for 10 minutes. Remove bread from oven after ten minutes, and carefully open the foil. Sprinkle tops of bread with mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Top with chicken parmigiana patties. Spoon extra sauce on top or save to serve on the side. Return bread to oven (not covered) and bake an additional 6-8 minutes until cheese is melted. Slice and enjoy immediately.
