One Pan Chicken Sausage with Roasted Vegetables and Apples

Source: https://www.spoonfulofflavor.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/One-Pan-Chicken-Sausage-and-Roasted-Fall-Vegetables_8172-300x450.jpg

Ingredients

  • 9
  • ounces chicken sausage,, sliced into 1/2 inch rounds
  • 1 1/2
  • cups broccoli florets
  • 1
  • cup halved brussels sprouts
  • 1
  • large sweet potato,, peeled and chopped into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1
  • large apple,, peeled, seeded and chopped into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 2
  • tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1
  • teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1
  • teaspoon fresh or dried rosemary
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Add all of the ingredients to a large mixing bowl. Toss to evenly coat. Transfer to a large, rimmed baking sheet. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until vegetables are tender, tossing halfway through baking. Serve immediately.
