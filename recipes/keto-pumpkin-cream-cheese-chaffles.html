Keto Pumpkin Cream Cheese Chaffles

Source: https://bloggingmomof4.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/keto-pumpkin-cream-cheese-chaffle-e1566351639924.png

Ingredients

  • 1
  • egg
  • 1
  • oz softened cream cheese
  • 1
  • Tbsp pumpkin puree
  • 1/2
  • tsp vanilla
  • 1
  • tsp confectioners Swerve
  • 1
  • tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • Pinch of baking powder
  • Shredded cheese I usually use colby jack

Directions

  1. Blend cream cheese, egg, pumpkin puree, vanilla, confectioners Swerve, pumpkin pie spice, and baking powder together. (if need be, you can put in a blender but I just combined with a fork).
  2. Following the basic chaffle recipe directions, spread a small amount of shredded cheese on the bottom of your waffle maker.
  3. Pour approximately 2 Tbsp of batter on top of cheese.
  4. Top lightly with more shredded cheese (careful to not overflow).
  5. Cook for 3 minutes and carefully remove to cool.
  6. Top with whip cream and a little pumpkin pie spice or even sugar free syrup.
