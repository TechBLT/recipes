Freezer-Friendly Mild Salsa (with Anaheim Pepper)

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs fresh ripe tomatoes (Roma or garden variety)
  • 1 cup yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 Anaheim pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (or 1/4 cup dried)
  • 1/4 cup lime juice (fresh or bottled)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. 1. Core and chop tomatoes. Optional: blanch and peel for smoother texture.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add onion, green bell pepper, Anaheim pepper, and garlic. Sauté for 5–7 minutes until soft.
  4. Add tomatoes, lime juice, salt, cumin, and sugar. Simmer uncovered for 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Blend to desired texture using immersion blender or in batches.
  6. Stir in cilantro. Cool completely.
  7. Portion into freezer-safe containers or bags, leaving 1 inch headspace.
  8. Freeze up to 6 months. Thaw and stir before serving.
