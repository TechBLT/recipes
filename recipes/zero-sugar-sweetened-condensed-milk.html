Zero Sugar Sweetened Condensed Milk

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2
  • cups heavy cream
  • 4
  • tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1
  • cup allulose (granulated)
  • 1
  • tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. 1. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, combine all ingredients and stir until blended.
  2. 2. Bring to a gentle boil, then simmer for 30–40 minutes, stirring occasionally—until the mixture reduces by about half and thickens.
  3. 3. Remove from heat and transfer to a glass container.
  4. 4. Chill for at least 30 minutes (or refrigerate overnight) to firm up further.
