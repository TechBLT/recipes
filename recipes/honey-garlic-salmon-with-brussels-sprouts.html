Honey Garlic Salmon with Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 5 salmon fillets
  • 2.5 cups Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2.5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1.25 tbsp honey
  • 1.25 tbsp coconut aminos or soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt and pepper. Roast 20 minutes. Mix honey, garlic, and coconut aminos. Brush salmon with glaze. Bake salmon 12–15 minutes until flaky.
