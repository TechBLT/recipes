Honey Garlic Salmon with Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 5
  • salmon fillets
  • 2.5
  • cups Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 4
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 2.5
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1.25
  • tbsp honey
  • 1.25
  • tbsp coconut aminos or soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Toss Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt and pepper. Roast 20 minutes.
  3. Mix honey, garlic, and coconut aminos.
  4. Brush salmon with glaze.
  5. Bake salmon 12–15 minutes until flaky.
