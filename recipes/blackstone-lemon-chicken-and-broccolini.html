Blackstone Lemon Chicken and Broccolini

Source: https://ifyougiveagirlagrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/IMG_1173.jpg

Ingredients

  • 4
  • tablespoons melted butter
  • 3
  • tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3
  • tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (I used thyme, rosemary, and basil)
  • 5 to 6
  • cloves garlic minced
  • 4 to 6
  • boneless skinless chicken thighs (or breasts)
  • kosher salt, pepper, seasoning blend of choice (optional)
  • 1
  • bunch broccolini
  • Optional: lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Mix together the melted butter, lemon juice, herbs, and garlic in a small bowl.
  2. Season the chicken with kosher salt, pepper, and seasoning blend of choice if using.
  3. Preheat the griddle to medium heat for a few minutes. Brush some of the lemon garlic butter on the griddle and add the chicken. Let the chicken cook 2 to 3 minutes then add the broccolini, season the broccolini with kosher salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the chicken and broccolini 7 to 9 more minutes, flipping both a few times. Brush both the chicken and broccolini with the lemon garlic butter a few times during the last few minutes.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges to squeeze over top if desired.
