Simple Steak Seasoning

Source: https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/C58IKU3tuFQ6RGjS6WYYLj80Mnk=/450x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc()/simple-steak-seasoning-recipe-336239-5_preview-5b05ef6f43a1030038538600.jpeg

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sea salt flakes
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients. Combine ingredients and place in an airtight container until ready to use. The recipe is enough for 4 to 6 steaks, depending on size. The mixture will stay fresh for up to 1 year if stored properly in a cool, dry place. To use, simply remove portion needed with a spoon and apply liberally to steaks. Grill, bake, broil, of skillet cook as directed.
