Keto Sausage Cheddar Biscuits

Source: https://www.ketofocus.com/wp-content/uploads/keto-sausage-cheddar-biscuits-1.jpg

Ingredients

  • Keto Sausage Cheddar Biscuits Ingredients
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup coconut flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pound ground sausage, cooked
  • 2 cups shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Mix together eggs, butter, salt and cream cheese. Add coconut flour and baking powder. Stir to combine. 4 Add cooked sausage and cheddar cheese and mix until combined. 5 Using a cookie scoop or measuring cup, scoop about 1/4 cup worth of biscuit mixture onto a parchment lined baking tray. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 to 18 minutes. 6 Let cool on the baking tray for several minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. To freeze, transfer cooled biscuits to a freezer safe bag and freeze. To reheat, wrap frozen biscuit in paper towel and microwave for 45-60 seconds.
