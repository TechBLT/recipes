Nona's Japanese Steakhouse Mustard Sauce

Source: https://img.sndimg.com/food/image/upload/q_92,fl_progressive,w_1200,c_scale/v1/img/recipes/18/57/16/MARqMceESnij4MRp7SnF_05B68BDE-5A37-4285-AD52-43EE911561D0.jpeg

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons dry mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 5 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons cream
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, lightly toasted
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Directions

  1. Using a blender or food processor, blend until smooth. This sauce freezes well. Itadakimasu!
