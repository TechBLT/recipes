Keto BBQ Sauce - Sweet & Low Carb with a Smokey Flavor

Source: https://www.myketokitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/keto-bbq-sauce-recipe-1-500x500.jpg

Ingredients

  • 16
  • oz sugar free tomato sauce
  • 4
  • tbsp worcestershire sauce Make sure theres no sugar on label
  • 4
  • tbsp apple cider vinegar With the mother
  • 4
  • tsp liquid smoke flavor
  • 2
  • tsp salt
  • 2
  • tsp onion powder
  • 1
  • tsp garlic crushed
  • 1/2
  • tsp cayenne powder Optional to taste
  • 1/4
  • tsp stevia powder Or your choice of sweetener, add slowly till taste.

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan.
  2. Place on stove over medium heat and lightly simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Allow to cool and jar or put in a serving dish
