Mediterranean Ground Beef Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1.25
  • lb ground beef
  • 1 1/4
  • zucchini, chopped
  • 1 1/4
  • cups cherry tomatoes
  • 4
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/4
  • tsp oregano
  • 5/8
  • tsp cumin
  • 2.5
  • tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet.
  2. Add garlic, zucchini, and tomatoes.
  3. Add spices and cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil before serving.
