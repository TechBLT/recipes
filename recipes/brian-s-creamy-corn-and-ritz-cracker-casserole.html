Brian’s Creamy Corn & Ritz Cracker Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz) whole kernel corn, drained
  • 2 cans (15 oz) cream-style corn
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed (about 30 crackers)
  • 1/2 sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed (for topping)
  • 4 tbsp melted butter (for topping)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all corn, sour cream, 1/2 cup melted butter, eggs, cheddar, 1 sleeve crushed Ritz, salt, pepper, and optional garlic powder. Mix well.
  3. Pour mixture into the baking dish.
  4. Mix the 1/2 sleeve crushed Ritz with 4 tbsp melted butter and sprinkle over the top.
  5. Bake for 40–45 minutes until bubbling and golden.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
