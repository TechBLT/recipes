Mike's Hot Honey Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • For the Hot Honey Syrup:
  • 1
  • cup Mike’s Hot Honey
  • 1/2
  • cup water
  • 10
  • dashes Angostura bitters
  • 6
  • dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1/2 orange (avoid white pith)
  • For the Cocktail:
  • 2
  • oz bourbon
  • 1/2
  • oz Hot Honey Syrup
  • 2–3
  • dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel
  • Optional: Luxardo cherry

Directions

  1. Make the Hot Honey Syrup:
  2. 1. Add Mike’s Hot Honey and water to a small saucepan over low heat and whisk until dissolved.
  3. 2. Add orange peel and simmer gently for 3 minutes.
  4. 3. Remove from heat and cool 10 minutes.
  5. 4. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters, strain, bottle, and refrigerate up to 4 weeks.
  6. Make the Cocktail:
  7. 1. Add bourbon, 1/2 oz Hot Honey Syrup, and bitters to a mixing glass.
  8. 2. Add ice and stir 20–25 seconds.
  9. 3. Strain over a large ice cube.
  10. 4. Express orange peel over the top and drop it in; add Luxardo cherry if desired.
