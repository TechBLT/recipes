Mike's Hot Honey Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • For the Hot Honey Syrup:
  • 1 cup Mike’s Hot Honey
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 10 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 6 dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1/2 orange (avoid white pith)
  • For the Cocktail:
  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1/2 oz Hot Honey Syrup
  • 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel
  • Optional: Luxardo cherry

Directions

  1. Make the Hot Honey Syrup:
  2. Add Mike’s Hot Honey and water to a small saucepan over low heat and whisk until dissolved.
  3. Add orange peel and simmer gently for 3 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and cool 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters, strain, bottle, and refrigerate up to 4 weeks. Make the Cocktail:
  6. Add bourbon, 1/2 oz Hot Honey Syrup, and bitters to a mixing glass.
  7. Add ice and stir 20–25 seconds.
  8. Strain over a large ice cube.
  9. Express orange peel over the top and drop it in; add Luxardo cherry if desired.
