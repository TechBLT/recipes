Hamburger Vegetable Soup

Source: https://img.moneysavingmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/Hamburger-Vegetable-Soup-500x800.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 2 cups peeled and diced potatoes
  • 2 cups sliced carrots
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 4 (14.5-oz) cans diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup brown rice, uncooked
  • 6 to 10 cups water
  • 1 1/2 to 2 1/2 Tablespoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 bay leaves

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef and onion together until meat is lightly browned. Drain off excess fat. Add all remaining ingredients and bring mixture to a boil. Cover and simmer for two to four hours. Check periodically and add more water, if needed. Remove bay leaves and serve. Makes 18 to 20 cups of soup. Can be stored in the refrigerator for up to four days. A pot will usually make enough for at least three meals for our family. We like it enough that we’ll eat it two or three days in a row!
