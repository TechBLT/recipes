Flavorful Slow Cooked Chuck Roast (Instant Pot, Upgraded Seasoning)

Ingredients

  • 1 chuck roast (3-4 lbs)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced (or 1 tsp garlic powder)
  • 2 onions, quartered
  • 4 carrots, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (or 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar)
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce or soy sauce
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar (extra, for balance)
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme (or 2 tsp dried)
  • 1 sprig rosemary (or 1 tsp dried)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced (optional)
  • 4 cups cauliflower rice or potatoes for mash
  • 2 tbsp butter

Directions

  1. Set Instant Pot to Sauté mode. Heat olive oil. Season roast with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and onion powder. Sear on all sides until browned, then remove. Add onions, carrots, mushrooms (if using), and garlic. Cook 2-3 minutes. Stir in tomato paste until darkened. Deglaze pot with red wine (or balsamic vinegar), scraping up brown bits. Return roast to pot. Add beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, rosemary, bay leaf, and extra balsamic vinegar. Cancel Sauté. Cover and set Instant Pot to Slow Cook on Low for 8-9 hours (or High for 5-6). When done, remove roast and shred or slice. Discard herbs and bay leaf. Steam cauliflower rice or boil potatoes. Mash with butter and salt. Serve roast with mashed cauliflower or potatoes, spooning pan juices over top.
