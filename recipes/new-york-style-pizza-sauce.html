New York Style Pizza Sauce

Source: https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0312/7695/7740/articles/new-york-style-pizza-sauce-517671.jpg?v=1675740598

Ingredients

  • 1 (28-ounce) can San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 small yellow onion, halved
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except the onion and butter in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Blend with an immersion blender until smooth. Pour the tomato purée into a medium saucepot. Add the onion to the sauce and cook on low for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. After 1 hour, use tongs to remove the onion from the sauce and discard. Add the butter and stir until completely melted.
