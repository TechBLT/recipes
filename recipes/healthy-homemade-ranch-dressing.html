Healthy Homemade Ranch Dressing

Ingredients

  • 3/4
  • cup plain Greek yogurt (whole milk for best texture)
  • 1/4
  • cup buttermilk (more to thin as needed)
  • 2
  • tbsp olive-oil or avocado-oil mayonnaise (or sour cream)
  • 1
  • tsp lemon juice or apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2
  • tsp Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1/2
  • tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2
  • tsp onion powder
  • 3/4-1
  • tsp fine salt, to taste
  • 1/4
  • tsp black pepper
  • 2
  • tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1
  • tbsp flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • 1
  • tbsp chives (or green tops of scallions), finely sliced

Directions

  1. Whisk yogurt, buttermilk, and mayo until smooth.
  2. Whisk in lemon juice, Dijon, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir in dill, parsley, and chives.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning. Thin with extra buttermilk 1 tsp at a time to desired consistency.
  5. Chill 30 minutes for flavors to develop.
