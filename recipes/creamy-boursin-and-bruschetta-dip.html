Creamy Boursin & Bruschetta Dip

Source: https://i0.wp.com/www.mrsbrookscooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/IMG_3639-scaled.jpg?w=1600&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 1
  • package Boursin Garlic & Herb Cheese
  • ½
  • tomato, diced
  • 3
  • garlic cloves, minced
  • 1
  • tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 2
  • tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh basil, julienned to garnish
  • Kosher salt, big pinch
  • Crackers or toasted bread, for serving

Directions

  1. In a small serving dish or bowl, press the Boursin cheese into the bottom, spreading it out.
  2. In another small bowl add the tomato, garlic, seasonings, 1 tbsp olive oil, basil, and salt. Mix until combined and spoon over the Boursin cheese.
  3. Drizzle the last tablespoon of olive oil, add more basil if desired, and keep chilled until serving. Serve with crackers or toasted bread, and enjoy!
