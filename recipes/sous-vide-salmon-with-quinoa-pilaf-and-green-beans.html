Sous Vide Salmon with Quinoa Pilaf and Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 4
  • salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 2
  • tbsp butter
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1
  • lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2
  • tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1
  • cup quinoa
  • 2
  • cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4
  • cup parsley, chopped
  • 1
  • cup green beans, trimmed
  • 1
  • tbsp butter
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. 1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Vacuum seal or bag with butter and dill.
  2. 2. Sous vide at 125°F for 45 minutes. Finish with quick sear or torch for color.
  3. 3. Rinse quinoa. In saucepan, heat olive oil and garlic 1 min. Add quinoa and toast lightly.
  4. 4. Add broth and bring to boil. Reduce to low, cover, cook 15 min until fluffy. Stir in parsley, lemon zest, and juice.
  5. 5. Steam green beans 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp. Toss with butter, salt, and pepper.
  6. 6. Serve salmon with quinoa pilaf and green beans.
