Ground Beef & Zucchini Bake

Ingredients

  • 1½
  • lb ground beef (80/20 or leaner)
  • 2
  • medium zucchinis, sliced into ¼-inch rounds
  • 1
  • small onion, diced
  • 3
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1
  • (24 oz) jar marinara or sun-dried tomato pasta sauce
  • 1
  • cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½
  • cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1
  • tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. 2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; sauté 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. 3. Add ground beef, breaking it up as it cooks. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
  4. 4. Stir in the marinara or sun-dried tomato sauce. Simmer 5–7 minutes to blend flavors.
  5. 5. Lightly salt the zucchini rounds and pat them dry after 5–10 minutes to remove moisture.
  6. 6. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, layer half the zucchini slices, half the meat sauce, and half the cheese. Repeat with remaining zucchini, sauce, and cheese.
  7. 7. Cover loosely with foil and bake for 25–30 minutes.
  8. 8. Remove foil and broil 2–3 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. 9. Let rest 5–10 minutes before serving.
