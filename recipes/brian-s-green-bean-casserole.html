Brian's Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • Green Beans (Sous Vide):
  • 4 lbs fresh green beans, trimmed into 1.5–2 inch pieces
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • Mushroom Cream Sauce:
  • 1.25–1.5 lbs mushrooms, diced
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 6–8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 5 cups turkey or chicken stock
  • 1.25 cups half-and-half
  • 1–2 tsp Better Than Bouillon Roasted Chicken
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1–1.25 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Optional: 1/4–1/2 tsp MSG
  • Topping:
  • French’s Crispy Fried Onions
  • Optional: 3/4 cup panko + 3 tbsp melted butter

Directions

  1. Sous Vide Green Beans:
  2. Preheat sous vide bath to 183°F.
  3. Add green beans to bags with butter, salt, and pepper; seal.
  4. Cook 45 minutes.
  5. Drain and set aside. Mushroom Cream Sauce:
  6. Melt butter and olive oil in a pan.
  7. Add onions and cook 5–7 minutes.
  8. Add mushrooms and cook until browned.
  9. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  10. Add flour and cook 2 minutes.
  11. Slowly whisk in stock, then half-and-half.
  12. Add BTB, soy sauce, Worcestershire, and seasonings.
  13. Simmer 5–8 minutes until thick and velvety.
  14. Taste and adjust seasoning. Assemble:
  15. Combine green beans and sauce in a 13×9 pan.
  16. Top with fried onions (and optional panko mixture). Bake:
  17. Bake at 350°F for 35–40 minutes (45–50 minutes if cold).
