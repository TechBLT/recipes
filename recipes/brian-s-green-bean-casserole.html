Brian's Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • Green Beans (Sous Vide):
  • 4
  • lbs fresh green beans, trimmed into 1.5–2 inch pieces
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4
  • tbsp butter
  • Mushroom Cream Sauce:
  • 1.25–1.5
  • lbs mushrooms, diced
  • 1
  • large yellow onion, diced
  • 6–8
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 6
  • tbsp butter
  • 3
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 8
  • tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 5
  • cups turkey or chicken stock
  • 1.25
  • cups half-and-half
  • 1–2
  • tsp Better Than Bouillon Roasted Chicken
  • 1
  • tbsp soy sauce
  • 1
  • tsp Worcestershire
  • 3/4
  • tsp black pepper
  • 1–1.25
  • tsp kosher salt
  • 1
  • tsp onion powder
  • 1
  • tsp garlic powder
  • Optional: 1/4–1/2 tsp MSG
  • Topping:
  • French’s Crispy Fried Onions
  • Optional: 3/4 cup panko + 3 tbsp melted butter

Directions

  1. Sous Vide Green Beans:
  2. 1. Preheat sous vide bath to 183°F.
  3. 2. Add green beans to bags with butter, salt, and pepper; seal.
  4. 3. Cook 45 minutes.
  5. 4. Drain and set aside.
  6. Mushroom Cream Sauce:
  7. 1. Melt butter and olive oil in a pan.
  8. 2. Add onions and cook 5–7 minutes.
  9. 3. Add mushrooms and cook until browned.
  10. 4. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  11. 5. Add flour and cook 2 minutes.
  12. 6. Slowly whisk in stock, then half-and-half.
  13. 7. Add BTB, soy sauce, Worcestershire, and seasonings.
  14. 8. Simmer 5–8 minutes until thick and velvety.
  15. 9. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  16. Assemble:
  17. 1. Combine green beans and sauce in a 13×9 pan.
  18. 2. Top with fried onions (and optional panko mixture).
  19. Bake:
  20. 1. Bake at 350°F for 35–40 minutes (45–50 minutes if cold).
