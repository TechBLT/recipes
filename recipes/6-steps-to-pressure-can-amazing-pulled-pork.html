6 Steps to Pressure Can Amazing Pulled Pork

Source: http://modernself-reliance.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/tw_jars-760x360.jpg

Ingredients

  • Canned Pulled Pork
  • ~1 lb of pork shoulder/butt (or beef)
  • 1
  • slices of bacon
  • 1
  • tsp liquid smoke
  • 1
  • tbsp salt
  • 1
  • tbsp pepper
  • 1
  • tbsp garlic
  • ~1/3 - 1/2 cup beef broth
  • pint jars

Directions

  1. As the water in the canner is coming to temperature, cut the meat in small cubes. You want to pack the jar as tight as you can. The meat will shrink as it cooks.
  2. Add bacon and all spices to the jar.
  3. Fill the jar with beef broth. Put lid on and tighten band completely. I like to give the jar a good shake to mix the spices and to let the broth settle into the crevices. I use my homemade broth, and you can have the recipe for my famous beef broth too .
  4. Remove lid and add more broth to leave 1" of headspace.
  5. Return band and tighten just until the threads catch as you would normally to can.
  6. When all jars are filled process at 10 lbs of pressure for 90 minutes.
  7. Hope yours turns out as delicious as mine! I love this method because we can open a jar and have a meal without having to cook a whole 4-5 lbs of pulled pork for just the two of us. Also it's shelf-stable for about a year.
