Brian's Gold Standard Sous Vide Turkey

Ingredients

  • 2 whole turkeys (approximately 21 lbs each)
  • HERB DRY BRINE:
  • 6 tbsp kosher salt (3/8 cup)
  • 4 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 tsp onion powder
  • 4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp dried sage
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
  • Optional: 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • FINISHING BUTTER:
  • 16 tbsp butter (2 sticks), melted
  • 1.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 1.5 tsp onion powder
  • 1.5 tsp dried sage
  • 1.5 tsp dried thyme
  • 0.5–1 tsp white pepper
  • Optional: 3 tsp poultry seasoning
  • Optional: 1–2 tsp bacon grease
  • EQUIPMENT:
  • Sous vide setup
  • Vacuum bags or freezer bags
  • Wire racks
  • Ice

Directions

  1. MONDAY NIGHT – BREAK DOWN & DRY BRINE:
  2. Break down both 21-lb turkeys into breasts (skin on), thighs, legs, and wings.
  3. Pat all pieces dry.
  4. Mix the herb dry brine ingredients together.
  5. Apply the dry brine lightly and evenly to all turkey pieces.
  6. Place pieces on wire racks, uncovered, in the refrigerator. Brine from Monday night until Wednesday morning (about 36–40 hours). TUESDAY – NO WORK: Allow turkey pieces to continue dry-brining and air-drying. WEDNESDAY – COOKING DAY: COOK DARK MEAT:
  7. Set sous vide bath to 165°F.
  8. Bag thighs (2 per bag) with 1 tbsp finishing butter per piece.
  9. Bag legs (2 per bag) with 1 tbsp finishing butter per piece.
  10. Sous vide for 12–14 hours.
  11. When done, transfer immediately to an ice bath for 20–30 minutes.
  12. Dry bags and refrigerate. COOK BREASTS:
  13. After dark meat finishes (or using a second bath), set bath to 145°F.
  14. Bag each breast individually with 1 tbsp finishing butter.
  15. Sous vide 2.5 hours.
  16. Ice bath 20–30 minutes.
  17. Refrigerate. END OF WEDNESDAY: All turkey pieces are fully cooked, chilled, and ready for reheating. THURSDAY – SERVE TURKEY #1:
  18. Set sous vide bath to 135–140°F.
  19. Reheat 2 breasts, 2 thighs, and 2 legs for 30–45 minutes.
  20. Remove from bags and pat dry.
  21. Torch or broil skin for 1–3 minutes until golden.
  22. Brush generously with finishing butter.
  23. Slice breasts just before serving. FRIDAY – SERVE TURKEY #2: Repeat the same reheating and finishing steps as Thursday. SUMMARY: • Dry brine Monday night → Wednesday morning. • Dark meat cooks at 165°F for 12–14 hours. • Breasts cook at 145°F for 2.5 hours. • Reheat at 135–140°F for 30–45 minutes. • Finish with broil/torch + finishing butter before serving.
