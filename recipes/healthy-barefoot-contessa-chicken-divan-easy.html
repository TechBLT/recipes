Healthy Barefoot Contessa Chicken Divan Easy

Source: https://foodievista.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Barefoot-Contessa-Chicken-Divan-2.webp

Ingredients

  • 5
  • tablespoons unsalted butter divided, plus more for greasing
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • 1
  • large head of broccoli cut into 1- to 2-inch florets, tender stems peeled and cut into ½-inch slices (about 5 cups)
  • 1
  • tablespoon chopped fresh sage
  • 5
  • tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3
  • tablespoons dry sherry or chicken broth
  • 1
  • cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1
  • cup milk
  • 1
  • cup heavy cream
  • 1
  • pinch of freshly grated nutmeg
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1
  • bunch of scallions chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 1 ½
  • cups shredded Gruyere cheese about 6 ounces
  • 3 to 3 ½
  • cups cooked diced chicken
  • ½
  • cup sliced or slivered almonds toasted
  • ½
  • cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼
  • cup breadcrumbs

Directions

  1. Preheat and Prepare:
  2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Grease a 3-quart casserole dish with butter and set aside.
  4. Cook the Broccoli:
  5. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil.
  6. Add fresh broccoli florets and cook for 2–3 minutes until crisp-tender.
  7. Drain and set aside. (If using frozen broccoli, ensure it is thawed before adding to the casserole).
  8. Prepare the Sauce:
  9. In a medium saucepan, melt 5 tbsp butter over medium heat.
  10. Add 1 tbsp chopped fresh sage and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  11. Whisk in 5 tbsp all-purpose flour, stirring constantly for 1–2 minutes until it turns a light golden color.
  12. Assemble the Casserole:
  13. In a large bowl, combine cooked chicken and cooked broccoli.
  14. Pour the creamy cheese sauce over the mixture and stir until well combined.
  15. Transfer the mixture to the prepared casserole dish and spread evenly.
  16. Top and Bake:
  17. Sprinkle the top with ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, ½ cup toasted sliced almonds, and ¼ cup breadcrumbs.
  18. Bake in the preheated oven for 25–30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbling.
  19. Serve:
  20. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
