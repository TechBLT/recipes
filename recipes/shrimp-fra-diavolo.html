Shrimp Fra Diavolo

Source: https://images.getrecipekit.com/20220225134752-recipes-2x1_shrimp.jpeg?aspect_ratio=16:9&quality=90&

Ingredients

  • 1/4
  • cup Olive Oil
  • 4
  • cloves, smashed Garlic
  • 16
  • colossal (U-12), peeled, deveined, butterflied Shrimp
  • coarse Salt
  • fresh ground Pepper
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1
  • 24oz jar Rao’s Homemade® Arrabbiata Sauce
  • 6
  • fresh leaves Basil

Directions

  1. Line a plate with paper towels and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large saute pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the garlic and cook until the garlic begins to shimmer in the pan.
  4. Using kitchen tongs, carefully place the shrimp into the pan and sauté for 1 minute on each side, or until the shrimp begin to turn opaque.
  5. Transfer shrimp to the paper towel-lined plate.
  6. Add Rao’s Homemade® Arrabbiata Sauce to the pan and bring to a simmer.
  7. Return shrimp and add basil to pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Serve immediately with more basil.
