Chicken Wings Dry Rub Recipe Alla Diane Lol

Source: https://www.windingcreekranch.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/dry-rub-for-smoked-chicken-1200-1200.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar, for sweetness
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder, for savory flavor
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder, for savory flavor
  • 1.1 /2 teaspoon garlic salt , for savory flavor
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika, for the red color
  • 1.1 /2 teaspoon chipotle powder, for spicy flavor
  • 1.1 /2 teaspoon black pepper, for spicy flavor
  • 2 tablespoon oil

Directions

  1. Place all the spices in a small bowl and stir to combine. Put oil and then spices i to gallon ziploc and massage or shake until each wing is completely covered. Cook in Air Fryer on chicken setting. However, seems best if it goes for at least 45 min.
