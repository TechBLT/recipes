DIY Vinegar Fabric Softener (Laundry Rinse Aid)

Ingredients

  • 3
  • cups distilled white vinegar
  • 1
  • cup distilled water
  • 20–30
  • drops essential oil (lavender, lemon, sweet orange, eucalyptus, or peppermint), optional
  • 1–2
  • tbsp vodka or rubbing alcohol, optional (helps disperse oils)

Directions

  1. 1) Add vinegar and water to a clean quart-size glass bottle or mason jar.
  2. 2) If using essential oils, first stir them into the vodka/rubbing alcohol (if using), then add to the bottle. (This helps the oils disperse.)
  3. 3) Cap and shake gently to combine. Label the bottle.
  4. 4) Use 1/4–1/2 cup per load in the fabric softener dispenser so it releases during the rinse cycle.
  5. 5) Do not mix with chlorine bleach. Avoid on rayon, acetate, and silk; spot-test delicates.
