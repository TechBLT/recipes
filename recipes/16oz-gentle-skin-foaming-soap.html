16oz Gentle Skin Foaming Soap

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp liquid castile soap (unscented, baby or sensitive version)
  • 1 1/2 cups (12 oz) distilled or cooled boiled water
  • 2 tsp jojoba or almond oil (light moisturizer)
  • 2 tsp vegetable glycerin (hydration boost)
  • 2 tbsp aloe vera gel (soothing)
  • 6–8 drops lavender or chamomile essential oil (gentle scent, optional)

Directions

  1. 1. Add the castile soap to a clean 16 oz foaming dispenser.
  2. Add jojoba/almond oil, glycerin, and aloe vera gel.
  3. Add essential oils if desired.
  4. Fill the rest with distilled water, leaving a little space at the top.
  5. Swirl gently to mix.
  6. Pump and enjoy — designed to be extra gentle and non-drying.
