Fiesta Cilantro Lime Wings

Source: https://www.intoxicatedonlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/FiestaCilantroLimeWings-14x20.png

Ingredients

  • 1 ½
  • pounds wingettes
  • 1
  • tablespoon lime juice
  • 1
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼
  • cup cilantro chopped
  • ½
  • teaspoon oregano dried; use approx. 1 tablespoon minced if fresh
  • 1
  • teaspoon salt
  • ½
  • teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼
  • teaspoon cumin
  • ½
  • teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients except the chicken wings.
  3. Add the wings and toss until they are completely coated with the marinade.
  4. Place chicken on a baking pan.
  5. Cook for 45 minutes, or until desired level of crunchiness is achieved.
  6. Top with additional cilantro if desired, serve.
