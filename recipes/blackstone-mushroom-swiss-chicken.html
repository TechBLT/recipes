Blackstone Mushroom Swiss Chicken

Source: https://ifyougiveagirlagrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/IMG_4855-1140x2016.jpg

Ingredients

  • 4
  • small thin cut chicken breasts (or 2 large thin cut pieces of chicken)
  • 1/4
  • cup Dale's Marinade and Seasoning (or store-bought marinade of choice)
  • cooking oil of choice
  • 8
  • ounces sliced mushrooms
  • kosher salt, pepper
  • 6
  • ounces chopped fresh spinach
  • 6
  • cloves garlic minced
  • 4
  • slices Swiss cheese

Directions

  1. Put the chicken in a gallon size plastic bag, add the Dale's or marinade of choice, massage the bag until the chicken is coated and marinate in the fridge for a few hours.
  2. Preheat the Blackstone to medium heat. Add some cooking oil, mushrooms, kosher salt, and pepper. Cook 5 minutes, mixing with spatulas.
  3. Add more oil to an empty side of the griddle and the chicken. Add the spinach and garlic to the mushrooms, mixing with spatulas.
  4. Flip the chicken after 4 minutes and add the spinach and mushroom topping to the chicken. Place a slice of Swiss cheese over top and cover with a melting dome for 3 to 4 more minutes.
