Flavor-Boosted Shepherd’s Pie

Ingredients

  • Meat Filling:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 cup beef broth
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary (or Italian seasoning)
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder (optional)
  • 1 beef bouillon cube or 1 tsp Better Than Bouillon (optional)
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • Mashed Potato Topping:
  • 2–2 1/2 lbs potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/3–1/2 cup milk or cream
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar or parmesan (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Boil potatoes in well-salted water until fork-tender, 15–20 minutes. Drain and mash with butter, milk or cream, salt, garlic powder, and optional cheese or sour cream. Set aside. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook 3–4 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Add ground beef and cook until well browned. Drain excess fat. Stir in tomato paste and cook 1–2 minutes until darkened. Add Worcestershire sauce, beef broth, salt, pepper, paprika, thyme, rosemary, onion powder, and bouillon if using. Simmer 8–10 minutes until thick and flavorful. Stir in peas and carrots. Taste and adjust seasoning. Spread beef mixture into a greased baking dish. Top evenly with mashed potatoes. Bake 20–25 minutes until bubbling. Broil 2–3 minutes for a golden top if desired. Rest 5–10 minutes before serving.
