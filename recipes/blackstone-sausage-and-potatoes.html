Blackstone Sausage and Potatoes

Source: https://bestblackstonerecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/BLACKSTONE-SAUSAGE-AND-POTATOES-RECIPE-16.jpg

Ingredients

  • 4
  • Russet potatoes peeled and diced
  • 1
  • lb. Smoked sausage sliced
  • 1
  • Large onion chopped
  • 3
  • tsp. Minced garlic
  • 1
  • tsp. Season all seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4
  • Tbsp. Butter

Directions

  1. Melt the butter over high heat.
  2. Add the onions, sausage, and potatoes.
  3. Sprinkle the seasonings over the contents.
  4. Mix it all together.
  5. Let it rest without stirring for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Then, stir and repeat until the sausage and potatoes are seared.
  7. Reduce the heat to medium, squirt water around, and cover.
  8. Continue to saute until the potatoes are done to your liking. Stir every once in a while to keep the mixture from burning.
  9. Remove the lid, stir in the garlic, and saute one more minute before serving.
  10. Enjoy!
  11. SHOULD I PRE-COOK POTATOES BEFORE GRILLING
  12. If you want to speed up the cooking process, you can.
  13. However, it is not necessary because when they are diced into 1-inch cubes, they will cook pretty quickly when covered.
