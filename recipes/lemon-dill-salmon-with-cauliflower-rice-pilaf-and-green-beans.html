Lemon Dill Salmon with Cauliflower Rice Pilaf & Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 5
  • salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 1
  • lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2
  • tbsp fresh dill, chopped (or 2 tsp dried)
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 4
  • cups cauliflower rice
  • 2
  • tbsp butter
  • 1
  • lb green beans, trimmed
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat sous vide to 125°F. Season salmon with salt, pepper, lemon juice, and dill. Seal in bags with a drizzle of olive oil.
  2. Sous vide salmon for 30–45 minutes. Remove and pat dry.
  3. Heat a skillet with olive oil. Quickly sear salmon for 30 seconds per side for color. Top with lemon zest.
  4. For cauliflower rice: Sauté garlic in butter until fragrant. Add cauliflower rice, season with salt and pepper, cook 5–7 minutes until tender.
  5. For green beans: Steam or blanch until crisp-tender, season with salt and pepper.
  6. Plate salmon with cauliflower rice pilaf and green beans on the side.
