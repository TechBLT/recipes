Sex on the Beach Drink

Source: https://mixthatdrink.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/sex-on-the-beach-recipe.jpg

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Peach Scnhapps
  • Grapefruit Juice (or orange juice)
  • Cranberry Juice
  • Lemon Juice (optional)
  • Hurricane Glass or any other tall glass
  • 2
  • ounces of vodka
  • 1
  • ounce of peach schnapps
  • 2
  • ounces of grapefruit juice (or orange juice)
  • 2
  • ounces of cranberry juice
  • Optional: teaspoon of lemon juice

Directions

  1. Feel free to tweak the ingredient amounts to your taste.
  2. Throw everything in a highball glass with ice.
  3. Stir.
  4. Garnish with an orange round and some cherries. Drink up!
