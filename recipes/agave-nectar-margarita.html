Agave Nectar Margarita

Source: https://img.sndimg.com/food/image/upload/q_92,fl_progressive,w_1200,c_scale/v1/img/recipes/36/90/80/picT8Boh5.jpg

Ingredients

  • crushed ice
  • 1 1/2
  • ounces of good silver tequila
  • 1/2
  • ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1/2
  • ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2
  • ounce agave nectar
  • 1/2
  • ounce triple sec or 1/2 ounce other orange-flavored liqueur
  • splash of fresh tangerine juice or clementine

Directions

  1. Fill a margarita glass with crushed ice.
  2. Add the tequila , lime and lemon juices, agave nectar, triple sec and tangerine juice to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  3. Shake vigorously and strain into the prepared glass.
  4. Garnish with a lime and orange wheel.
