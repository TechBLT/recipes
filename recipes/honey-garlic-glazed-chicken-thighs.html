Honey Garlic Glazed Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs (boneless or bone-in)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (rice or apple cider)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Steamed or roasted vegetables
  • White rice for serving

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. In a bowl, whisk honey, soy sauce, garlic, vinegar, and olive oil. Heat skillet or Instant Pot on sauté mode, sear chicken 2–3 min per side. Pour sauce over chicken, cover, and cook until done (Instant Pot: 8–10 min high pressure; oven: 400°F for 25 min). Simmer sauce until slightly thickened. Serve chicken with rice and vegetables, drizzled with extra glaze.
