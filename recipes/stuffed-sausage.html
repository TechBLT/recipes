Stuffed Sausage

Source: https://joyfilledeats.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/IMG_6038-500x500.jpg

Ingredients

  • 5 Italian sausage links about 1.5-2 pounds
  • 5 mozzarella cheese sticks
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400. Use kitchen scissors to cut a sausage down the side. Do not remove the casing. Put a mozzarella stick inside the sausage and press it back into its shape. Put it casing side down in an 8 x 10-inch baking pan. Repeat with the remaining sausage. Pour the marinara sauce over the sausage. Bake for 30-40 minutes until the sausage is 160 when checked with a meat thermometer.
