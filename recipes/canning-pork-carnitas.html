Canning Pork Carnitas

Ingredients

  • 1/2
  • teaspoon Cumin
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon Oregano
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon Salt
  • 1
  • Inch Bay leaf
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon Onion powder
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon Garlic powder
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon Orange juice Concentrate (the frozen tubes)
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon Lime Juice concentrate (optional)
  • 1/2
  • pound Pork cubed (shoulder, butt, loin)

Directions

  1. Cut pork into 1 inch cubes and place in canning jars.
  2. Top with remaining ingredients for each jar.
  3. Wipe rims of jars with vinegar
  4. Place lids and bands on jars.
  5. Place jars in pressure canner and vent for 10 minutes
  6. Add 10# weight (under 1,000ft altitude) See altitude chart if needed.
  7. Process 75 minutes for Pints and 90 minutes for Quarts.
  8. Allow canner to depressurize naturally
  9. Remove lid and allow jars to sit in canner with lid off additional 5-10 minutes
  10. Remove Jars from canner and set on towel/cutting boards
  11. Serving Ideas:
  12. Warm jar contents and serve in warm tortilla shells topped with sour cream, onions & fresh cilantro or serve over rice with toppings of your liking. Also great as the meat for loaded nachos. Enjoy!
  13. Sauce Directions:
  14. 1. In a frying pan add 1-2 tbls oil, 3-4 chili peppers, 1 onion, 3 garlic cloves, 1 bell pepper and all the spices. Cook until toasted 5-10 minutes . Onions & garlic should start to brown, chili peppers rehydrate and bell pepper softens.
  15. 2. Transfer to blender and add the reserve juice from the jar of canned carnitas. Blend veggies and juices adding 1-2 cups of beef broth to thin.
  16. 3. Transfer blended veggie to a pot on the stove and bring to a slow boil.
  17. Carnitas:
  18. Heat jar of carnitas meat in a skillet
  19. Dip flour tortilla in the chili sauce you prepared and fry tortillas in 1-2tbls oil.
  20. Spread shredded cheese on tortilla
  21. Spread warmed carnitas meat on top of cheese
  22. Top carnitas meat with raw chopped onions and chopped cilantro
  23. Fold tortilla in half and fry until golden brown and crispy on the outside
  24. Remove from heat, top with sour cream & cilantro or any of your favorite toppings
  25. Serve with a side of the chili sauce to dip your carnitas in and Spanish rice or beans for a complete meal
