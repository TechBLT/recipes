Classic Pozole

Ingredients

  • lbs boneless pork shoulder (Boston Butt)
  • tablespoon ground cumin
  • teaspoon garlic powder
  • teaspoon paprika
  • dash salt & pepper
  • 1/2
  • onion, chopped
  • tablespoons olive oil
  • garlic cloves, minced
  • cups chicken broth
  • (28 ounce) can pinto beans, undrained
  • (28 ounce) can white hominy, drained
  • (28 ounce) can fire-roasted tomatoes, choppped
  • tablespoon oregano
  • teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/8
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1/8
  • teaspoon pepper
  • tablespoon adobo sauce (more if you like spicy)
  • SERVE WITH
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • fresh cilantro
  • lime wedge
  • chopped avocado

Directions

  1. Rub pork with next four spices. Wrap in aluminum foil and cook on low in crock pot for 5 hours. Let pork cook then shred and set aside. In large pot, head oil and sauté onion until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook 2 minutes. Add tomatoes, broth, beans, hominy, oregano, cumin, salt & pepper. Simmer 15 minutes. Add pork and simmer another 15 minutes. Serve in bowls and garnish with desired toppings.
