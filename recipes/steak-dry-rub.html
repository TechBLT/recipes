Steak Dry Rub

Source: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2242610.jpg

Ingredients

  • 3
  • tablespoons kosher salt
  • 3
  • tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 2
  • tablespoons onion powder
  • 2
  • tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2
  • tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2
  • tablespoons coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1
  • tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 1
  • tablespoon ground cumin

Directions

  1. Mix kosher salt, smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, oregano, black pepper, brown sugar, and cumin together in a sealable container. Seal container and shake to mix.
