Sirloin Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 1
  • pack top sirloin, sliced thin
  • 1
  • onion, sliced
  • 1
  • cup mushrooms (optional)
  • 2
  • tbsp tomato paste
  • 1
  • cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 12
  • oz egg noodles or rice
  • 12
  • oz frozen Brussels sprouts
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. Cook noodles or rice according to package directions.
  2. Steam or roast Brussels sprouts with olive oil and salt.
  3. Sear sirloin slices, remove from pan.
  4. Cook onions and mushrooms until tender.
  5. Stir in tomato paste and sour cream.
  6. Return beef to pan, simmer briefly.
  7. Serve over noodles or rice with Brussels sprouts on the side.
