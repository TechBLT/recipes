Sugar-Free Old Fashioned Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup allulose
  • 1
  • cup water
  • 10
  • dashes Angostura bitters
  • 6
  • dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1/2 orange
  • Optional: 1 cinnamon stick or 1 clove

Directions

  1. Combine allulose and water in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until dissolved.
  2. Add orange peel and optional spice.
  3. Simmer gently for 3 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters.
  6. Strain, bottle, and refrigerate up to 3 weeks.
