Sugar-Free Old Fashioned Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup allulose
  • 1 cup water
  • 10 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 6 dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1/2 orange
  • Optional: 1 cinnamon stick or 1 clove

Directions

  1. Combine allulose and water in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until dissolved. Add orange peel and optional spice. Simmer gently for 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool for 10 minutes. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters. Strain, bottle, and refrigerate up to 3 weeks.
