BEAN WITH BACON SOUP

Source: https://www.timfarmerscountrykitchen.com/_files/ugd/3f5778_266548ae3826460f94ac51044d665eda.pdf

Ingredients

  • 1 cup navy beans
  • 2 tablespoons onion
  • 2 tablespoons celery
  • 2 tablespoons carrots
  • 2 strips bacon, chopped
  • 1 ⁄4 teaspoon hickory liquid smoke 2 teaspoons ketchup
  • Dash Nature’s Seasons
  • 2 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Pour all ingredients in clean and sterilized quart jar. Wipe top and place lid on top. Hand tighten. Put in pressure canner for 90 minutes (after the jiggler starts moving). To eat, add to chicken broth in saucepan and heat over medium heat, and serve.
