Healthy Blue Cheese Dressing

Source: https://thecleaneatingcouple.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/healthy-blue-cheese-dressing-1-676x1024.jpg

Ingredients

  • ½
  • cup nonfat greek yogurt
  • 1/3
  • cup blue cheese crumbles
  • 1
  • tablespoon lemon juice juice of one lemon?
  • ½
  • teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼
  • teaspoon pepper
  • 1
  • tablespoon milk
  • 2
  • tablespoons water

Directions

  1. In a food processor or small blender, combine all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
