Garlic Butter New York Steaks

Ingredients

  • 2-3 New York steaks
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups cauliflower rice
  • 12 oz frozen Brussels sprouts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions

  1. Season steaks with salt and pepper. Heat cast iron skillet or grill to high heat. Cook steaks to desired doneness. In last minute, add butter and garlic to pan, spoon over steaks. Sauté cauliflower rice in a pan with olive oil, season with salt and pepper. Roast Brussels sprouts with olive oil and salt until browned. Serve steaks with cauliflower rice and roasted Brussels sprouts.
