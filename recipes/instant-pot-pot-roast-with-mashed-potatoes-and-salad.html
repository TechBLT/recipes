Instant Pot Pot Roast with Mashed Potatoes and Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 chuck roast (3–4 lbs)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup red wine (or beef broth)
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 6 large russet potatoes
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Italian vinaigrette

Directions

  1. 1. Set Instant Pot to Sauté. Heat olive oil and sear chuck roast on all sides until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook 3–4 minutes. Stir in tomato paste.
  3. Deglaze with red wine, scraping up browned bits. Add beef broth, thyme, rosemary, bay leaves.
  4. Return roast to pot. Season with salt & pepper. Lock lid, set to High Pressure 70 min. Allow 15 min natural release.
  5. Meanwhile, boil potatoes until tender. Mash with butter and milk/cream. Season to taste.
  6. Toss salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber with vinaigrette.
  7. Serve roast sliced with mashed potatoes and salad on the side.
