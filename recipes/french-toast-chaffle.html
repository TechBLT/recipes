French Toast Chaffle

Source: https://beta.lazyketo.app/uploads/recipeimage_20191011200611586.png

Ingredients

  • 1
  • Egg
  • 1/2
  • Cup Mozzarella Cheese Grated and Chopped into small bits
  • 1
  • Tsp Vanilla
  • 1
  • Tsp Granulated Sweetener
  • 1
  • Tsp Cinnamon
  • 2
  • Tbsp Almond Flour

Directions

  1. Start by heating your waffle maker.
  2. Mix ingredients well and separate in half if using a mini waffle maker.
  3. Lightly spray waffle maker with oil and cook chaffles for 5 minutes or until desired crispness is achieved. Top with sugar free syrup. Makes 2 mini chaffles.
  4. We recommend using this waffle maker since it makes perfect bread slice size chaffles and most of the chaffle recipes will be for 2 of these mini chaffles.
