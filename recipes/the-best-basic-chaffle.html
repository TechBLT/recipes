The Best Basic Chaffle

Source: https://hangrywoman.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/MLA_0847-900x600.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mozzarella
  • 1 teapoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon almond flour
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Preheat your waffle iron. Mix together your eggs, spices, almond flour and baking powder. You want to be sure you mix them until they’re well incorporated. Thoroughly mix in your cheese of choice. For this chaffle I use mild mozzarella. Make sure that all of your cheese is coated evenly with the mixture. Pour the mixture onto your waffle iron and make sure to spread it evenly. Wait for the chaffle to cook in the waffle maker. It will become golden brown, and depending on your waffle iron it’ll take 5-10 minutes. Let the chaffle sit for 2-3 minutes until it becomes crunchy. Assemble your meal and enjoy!
