Brisket Rub

Source: https://heygrillheycdn.sfo2.cdn.digitaloceanspaces.com/2019/07/homemade-1-1.jpg

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons coarse Kosher salt
  • 2 Tablespoons coarse black pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 Tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 Tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Use a fork to crush any clumps of seasonings. This brisket rub can be stored in an airtight container for up to a month.
