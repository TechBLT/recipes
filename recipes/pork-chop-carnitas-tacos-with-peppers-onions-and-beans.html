Pork Chop Carnitas Tacos with Peppers, Onions & Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless pork chops
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 12 small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa

Directions

  1. Season pork chops with salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin, and paprika. In Instant Pot or slow cooker, add pork, chicken broth, and garlic. Cook on high pressure 25 minutes (Instant Pot) or 4 hours low (slow cooker). Shred pork with two forks once cooked. On a griddle or skillet, heat olive oil. Add sliced onions and peppers, sauté until softened and slightly charred. Warm tortillas on griddle until pliable. Heat black beans in a small saucepan until warmed through. Assemble tacos: tortillas + shredded pork + peppers/onions + optional toppings. Serve with black beans on the side.
