Ground Beef and Chopped Cabbage Skillet

Source: https://goo.letrecipe.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/FB_IMG_1758795190938-768x711.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1 small head of cabbage, chopped
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes (with juices)
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon bouillon powder (beef or chicken, your choice)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter

Directions

  1. Brown the Beef In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon. Drain excess fat if needed. Add Aromatics Stir in chopped onion and garlic. Cook for 2–3 minutes until fragrant and softened. Add Tomatoes & Seasoning Pour in the diced tomatoes with their juice. Stir in bouillon powder, paprika (if using), salt, and pepper. Let simmer for 5 minutes so the flavors come together. Add Cabbage Add the chopped cabbage to the skillet. Cover and cook for about 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cabbage is tender but not mushy. Taste & Serve Adjust seasoning to your liking. Serve hot as a complete meal or with warm crusty bread, rice, or mashed potatoes. Tips & Variations Swap ground beef for ground turkey, pork, or sausage for a different flavor. Add a dash of red pepper flakes for some heat. Sprinkle with shredded cheddar or mozzarella before serving for a cheesy twist. Make it heartier with cooked rice or quinoa stirred in before serving.
