Roast Pork in Apple Broth ~ Pressure Canning

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/E7497EA1-29AC-4316-913B-DAC19FC2A5F5.jpg?resize=100%2C100&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • Pork:
  • 8 1/2 to 9
  • lbs Pork shoulder, 2 inch cubes
  • 1
  • Tsp sea salt
  • Broth:
  • 2
  • Qts water or chicken broth
  • *
  • 2
  • Tbsp powdered chicken bullion if using water
  • 2
  • Qts apple juice or cider
  • 4
  • Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1
  • Pint apple butter
  • 2
  • Onions, Cut in half and sliced

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees f.
  2. Sprinkle pork with sea salt and roast @ 425 degrees f for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pot, bring all ingredients to a boil, turn down heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Prepare pressure canner, jars, lids and rings.
  5. Fill hot jars 2/3 full with pork, ladle broth in to 1 inch headspace.
  6. Wipe rims with white vinegar, put on lids and rings. Add jars to simmering pressure canner.
  7. Process 90 minutes for quarts, 75 minutes for pints.
  8. Enjoy!
