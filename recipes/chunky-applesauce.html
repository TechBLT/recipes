Chunky Applesauce

Source: https://luluthebaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/food-pix-307-480x480.jpg

Ingredients

  • 4
  • pounds apples, peeled, cored, and quartered
  • 1
  • cup water
  • 1/4
  • cup brown sugar
  • 1/4
  • cup white sugar
  • 4
  • big strips of lemon peel
  • juice from 1 lemon
  • 1
  • cinnamon stick (or 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon)
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot, cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until apples are soft, 20-30 minutes.
  2. Remove cinnamon stick and lemon peel. Mash apples with a potato masher until desired chunkiness is reached.
  3. Ladle hot applesauce into hot, sterilized quart jars leaving 1/2 inch headspace. Get out the air bubbles and wipe the rim of each jar. Put on hot, sterilized lids and rings.
  4. Process in a boiling water bath for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and cool completely.
