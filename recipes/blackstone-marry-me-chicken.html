Blackstone Marry Me Chicken

Source: https://ifyougiveagirlagrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/Keto-Friendly-Blackstone-Recipes.png

Ingredients

  • cooking oil of choice
  • 1
  • onion diced
  • 8
  • ounces sliced mushrooms
  • 2
  • pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs (or chicken breasts) cut into bite size pieces
  • kosher salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
  • 6
  • cloves garlic minced
  • 8
  • ounces chopped fresh spinach
  • 1/2
  • cup sliced sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1
  • cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2
  • cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1
  • pint heavy cream
  • Optional: basil for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat your Blackstone to medium heat. Add cooking oil of choice, onions, mushrooms, chicken, kosher salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Cook 6 to 7 minutes, mixing with spatulas a few times.
  2. Add garlic and spinach, cook 2 to 3 more minutes.
  3. Add sun-dried tomatoes, mozzarella, parmesan, and heavy cream. Mix with spatulas, containing the cream until it thickens into a sauce. Add more kosher salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning and cook 1 more minute.
  4. Garnish with basil if desired.
