Easy Homemade Pesto

Source: https://s23209.pcdn.co/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/EasyHomemadePestoIMG_8650edit-600x900.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts
  • ⅓ cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • ⅓ cup olive oil

Directions

  1. To make the pesto, combine basil, garlic, pine nuts and Parmesan in the bowl of a food processor; season with salt and pepper, to taste. With the motor running, add olive oil in a slow stream until emulsified; set aside. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
