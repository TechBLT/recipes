Sugar-Free Hot Honey Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • For the Sugar-Free Hot Honey Syrup:
  • 1 cup allulose
  • 1 cup water
  • 1–2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes (1 tsp = Mike’s level heat)
  • 8–10 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 4–6 dashes orange bitters
  • Peel of 1/2 orange (avoid white pith)
  • For the Cocktail:
  • 2 oz bourbon
  • 1/2 oz Sugar-Free Hot Honey Syrup
  • 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel
  • Optional: sugar-free cherry

Directions

  1. Make the Sugar-Free Hot Honey Syrup:
  2. Combine allulose and water in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until dissolved.
  3. Add crushed red pepper flakes and orange peel.
  4. Simmer gently for 4–5 minutes for Mike’s Hot Honey heat level.
  5. Remove from heat and steep 10 minutes.
  6. Strain very well.
  7. Stir in Angostura and orange bitters.
  8. Bottle and refrigerate up to 3 weeks. Make the Cocktail:
  9. Add bourbon, 1/2 oz syrup, and Angostura bitters to a mixing glass.
  10. Add ice and stir 20–25 seconds.
  11. Strain over a large ice cube in a rocks glass.
  12. Express orange peel over the top and drop it in; garnish with a sugar-free cherry if desired.
