Tuscan Beef & Rice Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2
  • lb ground beef
  • 1
  • jar Tuscan-style sun-dried tomato sauce
  • 1/2
  • jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2
  • onion, diced (optional)
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 1
  • tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2
  • cups cooked rice
  • 1 1/2
  • cups shredded mozzarella or Italian blend cheese
  • Grated Parmesan for topping
  • Garlic bread or salad for serving

Directions

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown the beef with onion and garlic; drain excess fat.
  3. Add Tuscan sauce and marinara, season with herbs, salt, and pepper; simmer 5–10 minutes.
  4. Combine with cooked rice and half the cheese.
  5. Transfer to a greased baking dish, top with remaining cheese and Parmesan.
  6. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Rest 5 minutes before serving with garlic bread or salad.
