Tuscan Beef & Rice Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar Tuscan-style sun-dried tomato sauce
  • 1/2 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 onion, diced (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella or Italian blend cheese
  • Grated Parmesan for topping
  • Garlic bread or salad for serving

Directions

  1. Cook rice and set aside. In a skillet, brown the beef with onion and garlic; drain excess fat. Add Tuscan sauce and marinara, season with herbs, salt, and pepper; simmer 5–10 minutes. Combine with cooked rice and half the cheese. Transfer to a greased baking dish, top with remaining cheese and Parmesan. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes until bubbly and golden. Rest 5 minutes before serving with garlic bread or salad.
