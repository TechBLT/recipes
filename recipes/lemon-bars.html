Lemon Bars

Source: https://bakingamoment.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/IMG_2527-best-lemon-bars-recipe.jpg

Ingredients

  • FOR THE SHORTBREAD CRUST:
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold (1 stick)
  • FOR THE LEMON FILLING:
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs, large
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest (zest of 1 lemon)
  • 2/3 cup lemon juice (juice of 3 lemons)
  • powdered sugar, for garnish

Directions

  1. TO MAKE THE SHORTBREAD CRUST Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F, mist an 8x8 pan with non-stick spray, and line with parchment. Place the flour, powdered sugar, and salt in a large bowl and whisk to combine. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan in an even layer, and bake for 20 minutes. TO MAKE THE LEMON FILLING Whisk the sugar, flour, eggs, lemon zest, and lemon juice together in a large bowl. Pour the filling over the partially-baked crust and bake for another 25 minutes, or until the filling is set. Cool completely, cut into squares, and dust with powdered sugar for garnish.
