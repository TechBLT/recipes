Low Carb Mock Snickers Bar

Source: https://cdn.ruled.me/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Mock-Snickers-Bar-1-featured.jpg

Ingredients

  • Shortbread Crust:
  • 1 ¼
  • cups almond flour
  • ¼
  • cup butter, chilled
  • ¼
  • cup granulated stevia
  • ½
  • teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 15
  • drops liquid stevia
  • Peanut Butter Caramel Filling:
  • ½
  • cup granulated Stevia
  • ½
  • cup heavy whipping cream
  • ½
  • cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chocolate Topping:
  • 6
  • tablespoons butter
  • 2 ½
  • ounces unsweetened chocolate
  • 2
  • tablespoons powdered Stevia
  • 2
  • tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 15
  • drops liquid stevia

Directions

  1. Shortbread Crust
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F
  3. Combine almond flour, butter, stevia, liquid sweetener, and xanthan gum in a food processor.
  4. Pulse until mixed into a fine crumby crust.
  5. Press mixture evenly into the bottom of an 8 inch baking pan
  6. Bake for 15 minutes until golden (make your filling and topping now)
  7. Set aside and let cool
  8. Peanut Butter Caramel Filling
  9. Combine the stevia with 1 tsp water in a small saucepan over medium-high heat
  10. Stir frequently until all the stevia has dissolved and the mixture bubbles, about 5 minutes
  11. Remove from heat and stir in the cream
  12. Return to heat and boil for 1 minute
  13. Stir in the peanut butter and vanilla extract until smooth
  14. Pour over the cooled shortbread crust and spread evenly. Let cool completely.
  15. Chocolate Topping
  16. Melt butter, chocolate, and stevia in a small saucepan over low heat.
  17. Stir occasionally until it has smoothed out and is glossy
  18. Add the cocoa powder and stir until there are no lumps
  19. Add the liquid sweetener and vanilla extract. Stir.
  20. Pour the chocolate over the cooled peanut butter filling that’s spread on the shortbread.
  21. Let set at room temp for at least 1 hour. You can add to the fridge after this but it hardens.
