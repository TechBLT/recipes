Traditional Italian Marinara (Freezer-Friendly)

Ingredients

  • 3
  • tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4
  • cloves garlic, sliced or finely minced
  • 4
  • lbs fresh ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped (Roma preferred)
  • 1.5
  • tsp sea salt
  • 1/4
  • cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Fresh cracked black pepper, to taste (optional)

Directions

  1. 1. Blanch tomatoes: Score the skins and boil for 30 seconds. Transfer to ice water and peel. Chop roughly.
  2. 2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant (do not brown).
  3. 3. Add peeled, chopped tomatoes and salt. Stir to combine.
  4. 4. Simmer uncovered for 30–45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
  5. 5. Stir in basil during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
  6. 6. Blend with immersion blender for smooth texture, or leave rustic.
  7. 7. Cool completely. Portion into freezer-safe containers and freeze up to 6 months.
