Peach Cobbler – Classic Sweetened Crust (9x13)

Ingredients

  • 8
  • cups sliced peaches (fresh or frozen, peeled optional)
  • 1/2
  • cup flexible sweetener (sugar, allulose, or honey)
  • 2
  • tbsp cornstarch
  • 1
  • tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2
  • tsp nutmeg
  • 1
  • cup all-purpose flour (or gluten-free blend)
  • 2
  • tbsp sweetener (sugar, allulose, or honey)
  • 1 1/2
  • tsp baking powder
  • 1/2
  • tsp salt
  • 1
  • cup milk
  • 1/2
  • cup butter, melted

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. 2. Toss peaches with 1/2 cup sweetener, cornstarch, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Spread in a greased 9x13 baking dish.
  3. 3. In a bowl, whisk flour, 2 tbsp sweetener, baking powder, and salt. Stir in milk and melted butter until combined.
  4. 4. Pour batter evenly over peaches.
  5. 5. Bake 40–45 minutes, until golden and bubbling.
  6. 6. Serve warm, optionally with ice cream or whipped cream.
