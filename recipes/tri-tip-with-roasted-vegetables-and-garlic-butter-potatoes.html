Tri-Tip with Roasted Vegetables & Garlic Butter Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2–3
  • lb tri-tip roast
  • 2
  • tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1
  • bag mixed vegetables (Costco or fresh mix)
  • 2
  • lbs potatoes, cubed
  • 3
  • tbsp butter
  • 2
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 2
  • tbsp parsley, chopped (optional)

Directions

  1. Sous vide tri-tip at 132°F for 2 hours. Remove, pat dry, and sear in hot skillet or on grill 1–2 minutes per side. Slice against the grain.
  2. For vegetables: Roast mixed vegetables on sheet pan with olive oil, salt, and pepper at 425°F for 20–25 minutes.
  3. For potatoes: Boil until tender. Drain, then toss with butter, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Serve sliced tri-tip with roasted vegetables and garlic butter potatoes.
