Pineapple Chicken and Rice: Sweet, Savory, Effortlessly Easy

Source: https://quickcookideas.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Pineapple_Chicken_and_Rice_Sweet_Savory_Effortlessly_Easy_2-768x960.webp

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast (or thighs)
  • 2 tbsp olive or sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tbsp honey or brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1.5 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 red bell pepper (diced)
  • 1/4 cup green onions (chopped)

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and chop vegetables. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add chicken to the skillet; cook until browned on all sides (6–8 minutes). In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce, pineapple juice, honey or sugar, vinegar, cornstarch mixed with water, and additional water; stir well. Pour the sauce over the cooked chicken; add rice, pineapple chunks, bell pepper, and green onions; mix gently. Reduce heat to low; simmer for an additional 5–7 minutes until heated through. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds and crushed red pepper if desired. Serving Size: 1 plate (about 300g)
