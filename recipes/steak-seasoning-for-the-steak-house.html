Steak Seasoning for the Steak House

Source: https://img.3lift.com/?width=600&height=600&url=%2F%2Fimages.3lift.com%2F11459991.jpg&logo_exclude=&v=17

Ingredients

  • 3
  • (1 lb) boxes kosher salt
  • 1
  • lb onion powder (sifted)
  • 1
  • lb garlic salt
  • 1
  • lb celery salt
  • 1
  • lb paprika (sifted)
  • 1
  • lb black pepper
  • 1
  • lb sugar
  • 1/2
  • lb nutmeg

Directions

  1. Mix well using a commercial mixer with a wire whisk if possible.
  2. This makes enough for 1 week in a 200 seat steakhouse.
