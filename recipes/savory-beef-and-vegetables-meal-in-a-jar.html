Savory Beef & Vegetables – Meal in a Jar

Source: https://www.canningdiva.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/savory-beef-pre-processed-1-scaled.jpg

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds hamburger, browned
  • 6 cups frozen peas
  • 5 pounds red skin potatoes, diced large
  • 6 Tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 large red onion, diced
  • 6 Tablespoons steak seasoning
  • 8 cups of beef stock
  • 8 cups of water
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large stock pot, add burger and season with salt and pepper, then brown on medium-high heat. Stir and break burger into small pieces as it browns. Drain fat in a colander and return browned meat to stock pot. Set aside. Rinse 6 quart jars in hot water to increase the temperature of the glass. Set quarts on a cutting board. Fill each quart jar half-full with browned meat. Tamp meat down packing it into the bottom of the jar. Repeat until each quart has tamped meat filling the bottom. To each jar add 1 Tablespoon of minced garlic and 1 cup of frozen peas, using a headspace measuring tool to evenly disperse the peas atop the burger. Fill the remaining space in each jar with potatoes, being sure to leave 1-1/4 inch of headspace. Push potatoes using fingers/thumbs so you can fit as many potatoes as you can so long as you keep a 1-1/4 inch headspace. Add a 1/4 cup of red onions and tamp down using your headspace measuring tool. Add 1 Tablespoon of steak seasoning (or your preferred seasoning of choice). To each jar, add 1 cup of beef broth, then add 1 cup of water until the water level covers the potatoes. Be sure to keep a 1-1/4 inch headspace. Wipe jar rims and bands with a warm wash cloth dipped in vinegar. Adhere lids and rings, hand tighten. Process quarts in a pressure canner at 10 pounds of pressure for 90 minutes. To the Table… When in need of a warm, healthy meal, simply pop open a quart, empty into a sauce pan, heat and eat! This goes excellent with a side salad and dinner roll.
