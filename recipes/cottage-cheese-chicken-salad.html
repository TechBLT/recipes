Cottage Cheese Chicken Salad

Source: https://thecookful.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/cottage-cheesee-chicken-salad-DSC_2996-square-1200-225x225.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup finely chopped cooked chicken
  • 2
  • Tbsp. low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1
  • Tbsp. finely chopped celery
  • 1
  • Tbsp. chopped green onion
  • 1/8
  • tsp. lemon juice
  • 1/8
  • tsp. salt
  • 1/8
  • tsp. black pepper

Directions

  1. Measure all ingredients into a medium-sized bowl.
  2. Mix well.
