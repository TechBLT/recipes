Easy Stevia Coffee Creamer

Source: https://www.crazycoffeebean.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/coffee-with-stevia-coffee-creamer-480x480.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup milk (or non-dairy milk substitute)
  • 1/3
  • cup heavy cream
  • 1/8
  • teaspoon liquid stevia
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients together in a jar.
  2. Shake the jar.
  3. Pour into black coffee or cold brew to taste.
  4. Leftover creamer can be refrigerated until the expiration date of the milk used to make it.
