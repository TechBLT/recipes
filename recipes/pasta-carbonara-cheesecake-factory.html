Pasta Carbonara Cheesecake Factory

Source: https://www.adventuresofanurse.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/pasta-carbonara-cheesecake-factory--150x225.jpeg

Ingredients

  • 6
  • slices thick bacon
  • 3
  • cloves garlic
  • 2
  • small onion
  • 16
  • oz spaghetti or fettuccini
  • 1/4
  • cup heavy cream
  • 3
  • eggs
  • 4
  • cups water
  • 8
  • oz parm cheese
  • 1/2
  • cup frozen peas (optional)

Directions

  1. For Stove Top Directions:
  2. In a skillet, cook bacon until crispy and remove from pan.
  3. Remove bacon from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add garlic and onion to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes in the bacon grease until tender and translucent.
  5. Remove bacon from skillet and set aside. Add garlic and onion to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes in the bacon grease until tender and translucent.
  6. Cook pasta according to package directions until Al’dente reserving a cup of pasta water.
  7. For the Carbonara Sauce
  8. While pasta is cooking, Make the egg mixture by mixing the eggs and parmesan cheese.
  9. Add 1 cup of the pasta water to the pan with the onions and garlic; stir in the egg mixture and heavy cream and whisk till creamy. Quickly Add the pasta while the pasta is hot and toss until well coated. Stir in half the bacon.
  10. Instant Pot Directions
  11. Place pot on saute, and cook bacon until crispy. Remove bacon.
  12. Continue cooking on saute; add onions and garlic to the pot and cook, scraping the bottom of the pan to remove any stuck bacon.
  13. Place Pasta and Peas(optional) inside the Instant Pot. Pour 4 cups of water over the top. Do not stir.
  14. Place on manual high pressure for 4 minutes.
  15. While Pasta is cooking in the Instant Pot, Mix the eggs and parmesan cheese.
  16. Do a Quick Release draining the pasta of excess water and reserving one cup of the pasta water.
  17. Then pour in the egg mixture and heavy cream. Toss until pasta is well coated.
  18. Add half the bacon to the pan and mix.
