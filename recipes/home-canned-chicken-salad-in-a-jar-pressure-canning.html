Home Canned Chicken Salad in a Jar!~ Pressure Canning

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/F1D05850-50AF-4754-B118-97212738E4E5.jpg?resize=530%2C530&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 4 Large chicken breast
  • 3 Allspice berries
  • 1 Tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1/2 Large onion, cut in 4
  • 2 Carrots, cut in 4
  • 2 Celery stalks, cut in 4
  • 1 Tbsp Chicken bullion powder
  • 6 Cups filtered water
  • 4 Tbsp white vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup prepared mustard
  • * 1 Tsp chicken bullion powder (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1/2 Large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Celery stalks, finely chopped

Directions

  1. Add all to a large pot. On medium heat, bring to a boil, turn down heat, cover and simmer 25 minutes. Pull chicken out and let cool. Strain poaching liquid and add 4 cups back into a clean pot, Save the rest just in case you need it. Prepare your jars and pressure canner. Get the canner simmering on low. To the broth add: Bring to a boil, turn down heat and simmer until ready to use. Dice the chicken breast small and add to large bowl. To the chicken add: Fill jars with chicken salad to 1 inch headspace loosely, do NOT pack. Add broth to 1 inch headspace remembering to remove air bubbles. Clean rims with white vinegar, add lids and rings and put in simmering canner. Process 75 minutes. Enjoy!
