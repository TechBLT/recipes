Roasted Green Chili Gravy for Pressure Canning

Source: https://i0.wp.com/jenigough.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/B94DD1EF-349C-4C4D-AFEE-FB7DE2148D0E-1.jpg?resize=512%2C530&ssl=1

Ingredients

  • 10 cups green chilis, roasted, peeled, seeded and chopped
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 14 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 18 cups water or chicken broth
  • 4 tbsp powdered chicken bouillon *If using water
  • 1 tbsp kosher or sea salt
  • 2 tbsp Mexican oregano
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp onion powder

Directions

  1. Inspect jars for cracks and chips. Wash jars, lids and rings in hot, soapy water. Prepare pressure canner per canner instructions, filling with water. Bring canner to a low simmer. Keep jars hot in hot water or dishwasher. In a large stock pot, cook onion and garlic in olive oil for 3 minutes on medium heat. Add in the rest of the ingredients. Bring to a boil, cover, turn down heat and simmer 10 minutes. Thicken with Therm Flo as directed if desired. Do NOT over thicken, mixture should not be as thick as “Gravy” and still pourable. Fill hot jars with hot gravy leaving 1-inch headspace. Wipe rims, put on lids and bands fingertip tight. put directly into simmering canner. Process quarts for 75 minutes and pints for 60 minutes.
