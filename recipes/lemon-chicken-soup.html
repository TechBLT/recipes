Lemon Chicken Soup

Source: https://thecleaneatingcouple.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/Lemon-Chicken-Soup-labeled-ingredients.png

Ingredients

  • 1
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2
  • cup finely chopped celery
  • 1
  • cup finely chopped onion
  • 1
  • tablespoon minced garlic about 3 cloves
  • 2
  • cups quartered carrots
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/4
  • teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2
  • bay leaves
  • 1.5
  • lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6
  • cups chicken broth low or no salt (1.5 quarts)
  • 1/2
  • cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • 1
  • cup dry orzo

Directions

  1. Stovetop Instructions
  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium high heat. Sauté celery, onion, garlic and carrots for 5 minutes.
  3. Once the vegetables have cooked, add dried spices, chicken breasts and chicken broth to the pot. Stir together.
  4. Cover the pot and bring to a boil. It should take about 10 minutes to come to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Once the soup has cooked, turn the heat back up to medium high.
  7. Remove the chicken breasts and set aside.
  8. Remove bay leaf and discard.
  9. Add orzo to the pot and stir together. Cook for ½ the recommended cook time on the package.
  10. While the orzo cooks, shred the chicken breasts with 2 forks.
  11. Once the orzo has cooked for half the recommended cook time, remove the soup from the heat. (The pasta will continue to cook as the soup cools.)
  12. Add shredded chicken and lemon juice to the pot. Stir to combine and enjoy!
  13. Crockpot Instructions:
  14. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium high heat. Sauté celery, onion, garlic and carrots for 5 minutes.
  15. Add sautéd veggies to the crockpot. Add dried spices, chicken breasts and chicken broth to the crockpot. Stir together.
  16. Place the lid on the crockpot and cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4-6 hours.
  17. While the soup cooks, bring a pot of water to a boil and cook your pasta per the directions.
  18. Remove the chicken breasts and set aside. Shred the chicken breasts with 2 forks.
  19. Remove bay leaf and discard.
  20. Add shredded chicken, lemon juice and cooked pasta to the pot. Stir to combine and enjoy!
  21. Instant Pot Instructions:
  22. Set instant pot to Sauté mode for 5 minutes.
  23. Heat olive oil over medium high heat. Sauté celery, onion, garlic and carrots for 5 minutes.
  24. Once the vegetables have cooked, add dried spices, chicken breasts and chicken broth to the pot. Stir together.
  25. Cover instant pot and set to sealing, ‘Soup’ OR Pressure Cook for 12 minutes.
  26. Allow the soup to come to pressure and cook. It should take about 10 minutes to come to pressure, but can take longer if your broth is cold.
  27. While the soup cooks, bring a pot of water to a boil and cook your pasta per the directions.
  28. Once the soup has cooked, quick release the pressure.
  29. Remove the chicken breasts and set aside. Shred the chicken breasts with 2 forks.
  30. Remove bay leaf and discard.
  31. Add shredded chicken, lemon juice and cooked pasta to the pot. Stir to combine and enjoy!
