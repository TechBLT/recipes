Garlic Butter Shrimp & Rice Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp paprika (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Roasted Brussels sprouts or green beans for serving

Directions

  1. Cook rice and set aside. Pat shrimp dry and season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Heat olive oil and 1 tbsp butter in a skillet; sauté garlic 30 sec. Add shrimp; cook 1–2 min per side until pink and opaque. Add remaining butter, lemon juice, and parsley; toss to coat. Serve shrimp over rice with vegetables on the side.
