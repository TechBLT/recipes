Garlic Butter Shrimp & Rice Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1
  • lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2
  • tbsp butter
  • 1
  • tbsp olive oil
  • 3
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2
  • tsp paprika (optional)
  • 2
  • tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2
  • cups cooked rice
  • Roasted Brussels sprouts or green beans for serving

Directions

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. Pat shrimp dry and season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Heat olive oil and 1 tbsp butter in a skillet; sauté garlic 30 sec.
  4. Add shrimp; cook 1–2 min per side until pink and opaque.
  5. Add remaining butter, lemon juice, and parsley; toss to coat.
  6. Serve shrimp over rice with vegetables on the side.
