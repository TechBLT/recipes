Homemade Liquid Laundry Detergent (Non-Toxic)

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup liquid Castile soap (e.g., Dr. Bronner’s Unscented)
  • 1/2
  • cup washing soda (Arm & Hammer Super Washing Soda)
  • 1/4
  • cup borax (optional – skip for ultra-sensitive skin)
  • 15–30
  • drops essential oil (optional – lavender, lemon, tea tree)
  • Warm water to fill 1 gallon

Directions

  1. 1. In a large bowl or jug, add 1/2 cup washing soda and 1/4 cup borax.
  2. 2. Pour in about 2 cups of warm water and stir until fully dissolved.
  3. 3. Add 1 cup of liquid Castile soap and essential oils if using. Stir well.
  4. 4. Transfer mixture to a 1-gallon container or jug.
  5. 5. Fill the rest of the container with warm water. Shake or stir well to combine.
  6. 6. Shake gently before each use (ingredients may settle).
