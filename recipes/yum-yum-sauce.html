Yum Yum Sauce

Source: https://lifeloveandgoodfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Yum-Yum-Sauce-4-1200x1800-1.jpg

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup mayonnaise
  • 1
  • tablespoon ketchup
  • 1
  • teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½
  • teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1
  • tablespoon mirin
  • ½
  • tablespoon rice vinegar

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, ketchup, garlic powder, and paprika.
  2. Add the rice vinegar and mirin, whisking until smooth. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
