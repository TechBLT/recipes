Low Carb Macadamia Nut Brownies

Source: https://cdn.ruled.me/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/macadamia-nut-brownies-featured.jpg

Ingredients

  • ¾
  • cup almond flour
  • ¾
  • cup erythritol
  • ¾
  • cup macadamia nuts
  • ¼
  • cup coconut oil
  • 5
  • tablespoons salted butter
  • 3
  • tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2
  • large eggs
  • 1 ½
  • teaspoons baking powder
  • 1
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1
  • teaspoon instant coffee

Directions

  1. The Execution
  2. Preheat oven to 350F.
  3. Cream butter, erythritol, and coconut oil together.
  4. Cream 2 eggs into the butter mixture.
  5. Add your cocoa, instant coffee, baking powder, vanilla extract, and almond flour slowly, mixing it in thoroughly.
  6. Add 1/2 Cup Macadamia nuts and mix well.
  7. Spread batter into a baking sheet and top with the rest of the Macadamia nuts, pressing them into the batter to submerge them.
  8. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool for at least 15 minutes.
