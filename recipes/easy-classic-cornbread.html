Easy Classic Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar (use 1/2 cup if less sweet)
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 large egg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the milk, oil (or melted butter), and egg. Stir just until combined; do not overmix. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 20–25 minutes, until golden and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool slightly and serve warm with butter or honey.
