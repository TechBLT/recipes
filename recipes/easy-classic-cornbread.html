Easy Classic Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1
  • cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1
  • cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3
  • cup sugar (use 1/2 cup if less sweet)
  • 1
  • tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1
  • cup milk
  • 1/3
  • cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1
  • large egg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the milk, oil (or melted butter), and egg. Stir just until combined; do not overmix.
  4. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 20–25 minutes, until golden and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Let cool slightly and serve warm with butter or honey.
