Easy Instant Pot Baked Beans

Source: https://www.centercutcook.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/easy-instant-pot-baked-beans-6.jpg

Ingredients

  • 6
  • slices bacon, chopped into half inch pieces
  • 1
  • small yellow onion, finely diced
  • 1
  • small green pepper finely diced
  • 2 – 16
  • ounce cans pork and beans
  • 3
  • tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 1/4
  • cup ketchup
  • 1/4
  • cup bbq sauce
  • 2
  • tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/4
  • cup brown sugar
  • 1
  • tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1
  • tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions

  1. INSTANT POT DIRECTIONS
  2. Select the sauté feature and cook bacon until browned and crispy. Use a slotted spoon to remove the bacon from the pot and set aside.
  3. Add in diced green pepper and onion and sauté until softened.
  4. Return the bacon to the pot.
  5. Add in remaining ingredients including pork and beans, yellow mustard, ketchup, bbq sauce, maple syrup, brown sugar, cider vinegar and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Close and lock the lid and select manual high pressure.
  7. Set the timer for 4 minutes.
  8. When the timer is done, quick-release the pressure.
  9. SLOW COOKER AND OVEN DIRECTIONS
  10. Cook bacon in the oven or on the stovetop until crispy.
  11. In a large non-stick skillet, sauté green pepper and onion until softened.
  12. In a large bowl, combine the bacon, sautéed green peppers and onions, and remaining ingredients including pork and beans, yellow mustard, ketchup, bbq sauce, maple syrup, brown sugar, cider vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce.
  13. If using a slow cooker or crock pot, transfer the beans mixture to the slow cooker and cook on low for about 4-5 hours or on high for about 2 hours.
  14. If using the oven, pour baked beans mixture into an oven safe dish. Cover and baked at 350 degrees F for about 45-55 minutes.
