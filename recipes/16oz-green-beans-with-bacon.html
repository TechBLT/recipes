16oz Green Beans with Bacon

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 4–6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 small shallot or ½ onion, finely diced (optional)
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1–2 tsp butter (optional, for richness)

Directions

  1. 1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Blanch green beans for 3–4 minutes until just tender but still bright green. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook chopped bacon until crisp. Remove bacon pieces with a slotted spoon, leaving some bacon fat in the pan.
  3. If using, sauté garlic/shallot in the bacon fat for 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the green beans to the skillet, tossing to coat in bacon drippings. Cook for 3–4 minutes until warmed through.
  5. Stir bacon back in, season with salt & pepper, and finish with a small pat of butter if desired.
