Mexican Rice (Rice Cooker Version)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth (or water + bouillon)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (or blended canned tomatoes)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional, mild)
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Drain well. Optional: Toast rice in skillet with olive oil until lightly golden, then transfer to rice cooker. Add rice, chicken broth, tomato sauce, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, and salt to rice cooker. Cook on White Rice setting. When done, fluff with fork and rest 5–10 minutes before serving.
