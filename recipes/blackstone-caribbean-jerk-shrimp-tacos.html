Blackstone Caribbean Jerk Shrimp Tacos

Source: https://ifyougiveagirlagrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/06/IMG_8851.jpg

Ingredients

  • cooking oil of choice
  • 1 red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper sliced
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 4 to 5 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons Caribbean Jerk dry seasoning blend (homemade or store bought)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • kosher salt, pepper
  • 6 to 8 soft taco size flour tortillas (or use lettuce wraps or keto friendly tortillas to stay low carb)
  • Toppings of choice: lettuce, cheese, sour cream, hot sauce, salsa, jalapenos, and/or store bought Caribbean Jerk sauce, etc.

Directions

  1. Preheat the griddle to medium heat for several minutes. Add some cooking oil of choice and the sliced peppers, and onions. Cook 3 to 4 minutes, flipping with hibachi spatulas a few times. Add the shrimp, garlic, Caribbean Jerk seasoning, lime juice, soy sauce, kosher salt, and pepper. Cook 3 to 4 minutes, stirring a few times with the spatulas. Add more cooking oil if needed. Remove the shrimp and peppers. Add the flour tortillas to the griddle for about 30 seconds per side to warm them. Serve with toppings of choice like lettuce, cheese, sour cream, hot sauce, salsa, jalapenos, and/or store bought Caribbean Jerk sauce, etc.
