Keto Sausage Breakfast Casserole

Source: https://www.ketofocus.com/wp-content/uploads/keto-sausage-breakfast-casserole-1.jpg

Ingredients

  • Keto Sausage & Egg Casserole Ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground sausage
  • 6 eggs
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • ⅓ cup coconut flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and grease a medium casserole dish with butter or cooking spray. Over medium-high heat, cook olive oil and sausage in a large skillet until the sausage is cooked through. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool slightly. 3 Whisk together eggs, heavy cream, salt and pepper into a large bowl. Add in coconut flour, baking powder, green onions and shredded cheddar cheese (reserving about ½ cup for the topping). Mix until combined. Stir the cooked sausage to the egg mixture. Spread into the casserole dish. Top with remaining ½ cup of shredded cheddar cheese. Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until the casserole is golden brown on top and the center is set. Cook the pork sausage. Mix all ingredients (including cook sausage) to a large bowl. Spread into a baking dish and bake for 30 minutes!
