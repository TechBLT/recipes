Sous Vide Greek Pork Tenderloin Kebabs

Ingredients

  • 2 pork tenderloins (about 2 lbs total), trimmed
  • 1 medium red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 bell pepper (optional), cut into chunks
  • 8 metal or soaked wooden skewers
  • Marinade:
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (fresh)
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions

  1. 1. Cut pork tenderloin into large 2-inch chunks. Pat dry.
  2. In a bowl, whisk olive oil, yogurt, lemon juice, vinegar, garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss pork in marinade. Marinate 30–60 minutes.
  3. Transfer pork and marinade to a vacuum bag or heavy-duty freezer bag. Sous vide at 135°F (57°C) for 1 1/2–2 hours (or 140°F/60°C for slightly firmer texture).
  4. Remove pork, discard liquid, and pat cubes very dry.
  5. Thread pork, onion, and bell pepper onto skewers. Brush lightly with olive oil.
  6. Preheat grill or cast iron griddle to high heat. Sear skewers 1–2 minutes per side until browned and slightly charred.
  7. Rest 3–5 minutes before serving. Serve with lemon wedges, tzatziki, or warm pita.
