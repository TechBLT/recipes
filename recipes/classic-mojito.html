Classic Mojito

Ingredients

  • 10–12 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • Club soda (to top)
  • Crushed ice
  • Mint sprig and lime wheel (for garnish)

Directions

  1. 1. Add mint leaves and simple syrup to a tall glass. Gently muddle to release oils.
  2. Add rum and lime juice.
  3. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
  4. Top with club soda and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wheel.
