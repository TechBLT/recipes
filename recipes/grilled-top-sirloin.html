Grilled Top Sirloin

Ingredients

  • 1 pack top sirloin steaks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 12 oz frozen Brussels sprouts
  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce

Directions

  1. Season steaks with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-sear to preferred doneness. Roast Brussels sprouts with olive oil and salt until browned. Cook pasta according to package directions. Heat marinara sauce separately. Serve steaks with roasted Brussels sprouts and pasta with marinara.
